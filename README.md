### 理解高中对长期结果的影响
**[Understanding High Schools’ Effects on Longer-Term Outcomes](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34913)**

**Authors**: Preeya P. Mbekeani, John P. Papay, Ann Mantil, Richard J. Murnane

**Abstract**: Improving education and labor market outcomes for low-income students is critical for advancing socioeconomic mobility in the United States. We use longitudinal data on five cohorts of 9th grade students to explore how Massachusetts public high schools affect the longer-term outcomes of students, with a special focus on students from low-income families. Using detailed administrative and student survey data, we estimate school value-added impacts on college outcomes and earnings. Observationally similar students who attend a school at the 80th percentile of the value-added distribution instead of a school at the 20th percentile are 11% more likely to enroll in college, are 31% more likely to graduate from a four-year college, and earn 25% (or $10,500) more annually at age 30. On average, schools that improve students’ longer-run outcomes the most are those that improve their 10th grade test scores and increase their college plans the most.

**摘要**: 提高低收入学生的教育和劳动市场结果对于推进美国的经济社会流动性至关重要。我们使用五个9年级学生队列的纵向数据，探索马萨诸塞州公立高中如何影响学生的长期结果，特别关注来自低收入家庭的学生。利用详细的行政和学生调查数据，我们估计学校对大学结果和收入的价值增加影响。在价值增加分布的第80百分位而不是第20百分位的学校就读的观察相似学生，上大学的可能性高11%，从四年制大学毕业的可能性高31%，并且在30岁时每年多赚25%（或10500美元）。平均而言，最改善学生长期结果的学校是那些最改善他们的10年级考试成绩和最增加他们的大学计划的学校。

### 孤独、心理健康与在家工作革命
**[Loneliness, Mental Health and the Work-From-Home Revolution](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34914)**

**Authors**: Benjamin W. Cowan, Joe M. Spearing

**Abstract**: The large increase in remote work since 2020 has prompted concerns about adverse effects on population loneliness and mental health. We show that any such adverse effects were small, in a UK context. We use data from UKHLS and differences-in-differences estimators that flexibly control for a rich set of covariates to compare changes in key variables amongst two groups: those who worked in teleworkable occupations in 2019, and those who worked in non-teleworkable occupations in 2019. While the former experience large and persistent increases in their probability of working remotely compared to the latter, any relative changes in self-reported loneliness or adverse mental health symptoms are transitory and disappear by the year 2023.

**摘要**: 自2020年以来，远程工作的大幅增加引发了人们对人口孤独感和心理健康负面影响的担忧。我们在英国背景下表明，任何这样的负面影响都是微小的。我们使用了来自英国家庭纵向研究的数据和差异中的差异估计器，灵活地控制了一系列丰富的协变量，比较了两组人群在关键变量上的变化：2019年从事可远程工作职业的人群和2019年从事不可远程工作职业的人群。虽然前者与后者相比，远程工作的可能性大幅且持续增加，但自我报告的孤独感或不良心理健康症状的相对变化是短暂的，并在2023年消失。

### 提高器官采购操作
**[Improving Organ Procurement Operations](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34890)**

**Authors**: Hammaad Adam, Nikhil Agarwal, Marzyeh Ghassemi

**Abstract**: We study how decisions made by organ procurement organizations (OPOs)—non-profits that coordinate organ recovery from deceased donors—affect the availability of organs for transplant in the United States. We develop a structural econometric model of a pivotal OPO decision: whether to approach a potential donor’s family to request authorization for donation. Our model conceptualizes this decision in two parts. The OPO first estimates the probabilities of two downstream outcomes: authorization (i.e., family consent) and transplant (i.e., whether the donated organs would be successfully transplanted). It then applies a cost-benefit decision rule that maps these estimates to an approach decision. Our model separately identifies the OPO’s beliefs (i.e., probability estimates), its preferences (i.e., costs / benefits), and the true probabilities of authorization and transplant. We apply our model to a dataset of 35,856 potential donors referred to four OPOs between 2016 and 2021. We find that OPOs missed a substantial number of donation opportunities, recovering organs from only 39% of transplantable donors. Of these missed opportunities, an estimated 16% resulted from conservative preferences, 12% from miscalibrated beliefs, and the remaining 72% from family declines. We conduct a detailed counterfactual policy evaluation to identify impactful and actionable changes to OPO decisions. We find that drastically increasing approach rates would allow OPOs to recover organs from 43% more donors, creating over $100 million in additional annual societal benefit.

**摘要**: 我们研究了器官采购组织（OPOs）——这些非营利组织负责协调从已故捐献者那里回收器官——所做的决策如何影响美国可用于移植的器官供应。我们开发了一个结构性计量经济学模型，用于分析OPO的关键决策：是否接触潜在捐献者的家属，请求授权捐献。我们的模型将这一决策概念化为两部分。首先，OPO估计两个下游结果的概率：授权（即家属同意）和移植（即捐献的器官是否成功移植）。然后，它应用成本效益决策规则，将这些估计值映射到接触决策上。我们的模型分别识别了OPO的信念（即概率估计）、偏好（即成本/收益）以及授权和移植的真实概率。我们将模型应用于2016年至2021年间向四个OPO推荐的35,856名潜在捐献者的数据集。我们发现OPO错过了大量捐献机会，仅从39%的可移植捐献者那里回收了器官。在这些错过的机会中，估计有16%是由于保守的偏好，12%是由于信念失调，其余72%是由于家属拒绝。我们进行了详细的反事实政策评估，以确定对OPO决策有影响且可操作的变革。我们发现，大幅提高接触率将使OPO能够从43%更多的捐献者那里回收器官，创造超过1亿美元的额外年度社会福利。

### 求职者的在线伙伴：一项实地实验
**[Online Buddies for Job Seekers: A Field Experiment](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34912)**

**Authors**: Bart K. de Koning, Paul Muller, Michèle Belot, Yvonne Engels, Didier Fouarge, Mario Keer, Philipp Kircher, Sandra Phlippen

**Abstract**: We design an online platform to connect unemployed job seekers with ‘buddies’: former job seekers who recently found employment. We focus on job seekers who search in occupations with poor prospects and buddies who successfully switched occupations. In a randomized controlled trial, we evaluate the impact of access to the platform on labor market outcomes. We find sizable effects. Thirteen to 18 months after getting access, initially unemployed job seekers are 6 percentage points (11%) more likely to be employed and earn e 226 more per month than those without access. The positive impact is concentrated among the long-term unemployed.

**摘要**: 我们设计了一个在线平台，将失业的求职者与“伙伴”联系起来：“伙伴”是最近找到工作的前求职者。我们专注于那些在前景不佳的职业中寻找工作的求职者，以及成功转换职业的伙伴。在一项随机对照试验中，我们评估了接触该平台对劳动市场结果的影响。我们发现有相当大的效果。在获得访问权限后的13到18个月，最初失业的求职者比那些没有访问权限的人更有可能就业（高出6个百分点，即11%），并且每月多赚226欧元。积极影响集中在长期失业者中。

### 消费楔子：测量和诊断扭曲
**[Consumption Wedges: Measuring and Diagnosing Distortions](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34891)**

**Authors**: Sasha Indarte, Raymond Kluender, Ulrike Malmendier, Michael Stepner

**Abstract**: Ample empirical evidence documents deviations from the canonical consumption-savings model; yet, it remains difficult to assess the roles of different underlying distortions, such as financial constraints and behavioral preferences. We develop a sufficient-statistics approach that measures individual-level wedges between observed and counterfactual “frictionless” consumption. Since different distortions imply different wedge properties, wedges provide a diagnostic to distinguish between models. We measure wedges using administrative transactions data linked to surveyed expectations for a population of middle-income, low-liquidity US consumers. The expectations data allow us to distinguish wedges attributable to frictions and behavioral preferences from wedges driven by deviations from full-information rational expectations (FIRE). We find that consumption wedges are large and heterogeneous: the median wedge is 40% of frictionless consumption in absolute value, with 49% of consumers under-consuming (negative wedges) and 51% over-consuming (positive wedges). Borrowing constraints cannot rationalize this pattern because they only generate negative wedges. Models combining present bias with borrowing constraints, or featuring consumption adjustment costs, best account for the wedge properties we document.

**摘要**: 大量的实证证据记录了与经典消费储蓄模型的偏差；然而，评估不同潜在扭曲（如金融约束和行为偏好）的作用仍然很困难。我们开发了一种充分统计方法，用于测量个体层面观察到的与反事实“无摩擦”消费之间的差异。由于不同的扭曲意味着不同的差异属性，差异提供了一种区分不同模型的诊断工具。我们使用与调查预期相关联的行政交易数据来衡量美国中等收入、流动性低的消费者群体的差异。预期数据使我们能够区分由摩擦和行为偏好导致的差异，以及由偏离完全信息理性预期（FIRE）导致的差异。我们发现消费差异很大且异质性高：中位数差异是无摩擦消费的40%（绝对值），有49%的消费者消费不足（负差异），51%的消费者消费过度（正差异）。借款约束无法解释这种模式，因为它们只产生负差异。结合现在偏见和借款约束的模型，或具有消费调整成本的模型，最能解释我们记录的差异属性。

### 利率风险与微观审慎监管的横截面效应
**[Interest Rate Risk and Cross-Sectional Effects of Micro-Prudential Regulation](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34892)**

**Authors**: Juliane Begenau, Vadim Elenev, Tim Landvoigt

**Abstract**: This paper investigates financial stability risks arising from banks' interest rate exposure and uninsured deposit funding. We develop a model of heterogeneous banks featuring endogenous run risk to jointly analyze portfolio and funding choices. The model replicates key empirical patterns, including the concentration of uninsured deposits in larger banks. We analyze the impact of monetary policy rate hikes and evaluate the capacity of microprudential tools to mitigate bank fragility. Results demonstrate that tightening capital requirements significantly lowers run risk. Higher liquidity requirements targeting uninsured deposits efficiently reduce run risk, provided they are met exclusively with reserves.

**摘要**: 本文研究了银行利率风险敞口和未保险存款资金所带来的金融稳定性风险。我们构建了一个具有内生挤兑风险的异质性银行模型，以联合分析投资组合和资金选择。该模型复制了关键的实证模式，包括未保险存款在较大银行中的集中。我们分析了货币政策利率上调的影响，并评估了微观审慎工具减轻银行脆弱性的能力。结果表明，收紧资本要求显著降低了挤兑风险。针对未保险存款的更高流动性要求有效地降低了挤兑风险，前提是它们完全用准备金来满足。

### 希望之光？中国与太阳能的崛起
**[Ray of Hope? China and the Rise of Solar Energy](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34893)**

**Authors**: Ignacio Banares-Sanchez, Robin Burgess, Dávid László, Pol Simpson, John Van Reenen, Yifan Wang

**Abstract**: Do industrial policies that promote clean energy offer a “ray of hope”, increasing a country’s growth and welfare, whilst simultaneously reducing carbon emissions? We study the impact of Chinese solar subsidies whose implementation by city-regions went alongside massive expansion of the sector and a dramatic fall in global solar prices. We construct new city and firm panel data on solar policies, patenting and output. Using synthetic-difference-in-differences 2004-2020, we find production and innovation subsidies were more effective than demand-side (installation) subsidies in generating large and persistent increases in local innovation, net entry, production and exports. Demand policies did, however, reduce local pollution. To examine aggregate effects, we build and structurally estimate a quantitative spatial model with endogenous innovation and heterogeneous productivity across firms and cities, which accounts for business stealing and knowledge spillovers. Counterfactual analysis shows that: (i) local effects remain substantial at the macro level explaining 40%-50% of the aggregate changes in solar innovation, prices and revenues; (ii) social benefits to Chinese citizens exceed subsidy costs by 65% (and double this when environmental benefits are included); and (iii) although all subsidy types increase welfare, innovation subsidies are the most cost-effective.

**摘要**: 推动清洁能源的产业政策是否提供了一线希望，既增加了一个国家的增长和福利，同时又减少了碳排放？我们研究了中国太阳能补贴的影响，这些补贴的实施与该行业的大规模扩张和全球太阳能价格的戏剧性下降同时发生。我们构建了关于太阳能政策、专利和产出的新的城市和公司面板数据。使用2004-2020年的合成差异-差异方法，我们发现生产和创新补贴比需求侧（安装）补贴更有效，能够产生大且持久的当地创新、净进入、生产和出口增长。然而，需求政策确实减少了当地污染。为了检验总体效应，我们构建并结构化估计了一个量化的空间模型，该模型具有内生创新和公司及城市间的异质性生产力，同时考虑了商业窃取和知识溢出效应。反事实分析表明：（i）在宏观层面上，地方效应仍然显著，解释了太阳能创新、价格和收入总体变化的40%-50%；（ii）中国公民的社会福利超出补贴成本的65%（当包括环境效益时，这一数字翻倍）；以及（iii）尽管所有类型的补贴都增加了福利，但创新补贴最具成本效益。

### 通货膨胀与包容性：大流行之后的稳定政策
**[Inflation vs Inclusion: Stabilization Policy in the Wake of the Pandemic](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34894)**

**Authors**: Felipe Alves, Giovanni L. Violante

**Abstract**: As the economy emerges from a crisis, macroeconomic policy confronts a dilemma: a protracted stimulus can foster a more inclusive labor market recovery, yet risks igniting inflation that ultimately undermines workers’ welfare through real income erosion. This tension amplifies in the presence of the ZLB and aggregate capacity constraints. We embed this insight into a quantitative model of the US economy. We study how monetary and fiscal policies managed this inflation-inclusion trade-off after the pandemic, contrasting actual outcomes with counterfactual scenarios. Our experiments yield five findings: (i) the trade-off was unusually difficult because policy was squeezed between these two constraints; (ii) inflationary pressures arose from the joint deployment of prolonged monetary and fiscal stimulus; either policy alone would have produced milder price dynamics; (iii) either inclusive fiscal policy or inclusive monetary policy in isolation would have been sufficient to contain the negative labor market hysteresis at the bottom of the distribution; (iv) inclusive fiscal policy combined with a more traditionally inflation-focused central bank would have achieved higher welfare for the vast majority of households; (v) welfare effects reflect mostly corrections of incomplete-market inefficiencies rather than gains from aggregate stabilization.

**摘要**: 尽管金融理论区分了股权和债务在支持公司增长方面的作用，它们对国际贸易的不同影响尚未得到充分研究。本研究首次提供了实证分析，探讨了获得股权融资如何影响公司的出口业务。我们利用中国独特的制度环境，即首次公开募股（IPO）需要严格的监管审批，确保只有合格的公司才能进入最终审查阶段。我们的实证策略比较了成功IPO申请者的出口表现与“接近成功”的申请者——在最终审查会议中被拒绝的申请者的出口表现。为了加强识别，我们利用会议记录排除了因对未来收入增长或盈利风险的担忧而被拒绝的情况，因为这些可能涉及对出口表现的未观察到的冲击。我们基于队列的双重差分分析显示，IPO批准导致公司出口在接下来的六年中每年增长超过6%。与之前关于债务融资的发现不同，IPO批准主要影响的是广泛边际，使公司能够扩展到更多的目标产品市场。机制测试表明，IPO通过资助无形投资和促进风险承担活动来增强出口。

### 政府政策的效率、保险和再分配效应
**[Efficiency, Insurance, and Redistribution Effects of Government Policies](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34907)**

**Authors**: Anmol Bhandari, David Evans, Mikhail Golosov, Thomas J. Sargent

**Abstract**: This paper decomposes welfare measures of policy reforms into parts attributable to redistribution and parts due to efficiency. We further decompose efficiency into subcomponents such as gains from better insurance against idiosyncratic and aggregate risk. Our decomposition of welfare measures associated with alternative feasible allocations is cast in terms of a coordinate system that uses generalized Pareto–Negishi weights to capture inequality and production and consumption wedges to capture distortions. Our decomposition has several desirable properties. It attributes welfare changes from movements along a Pareto frontier to redistribution; it attributes negative efficiency changes to movements away from the Pareto frontier; and it produces subcomponent shares of welfare changes that are numeraire-invariant and symmetric with respect to the direction of the reform. Our decomposition can be explained in terms of an implicit tax-and-transfer system in which redistribution captures real income changes, efficiency captures deadweight losses, and output costs.

**摘要**: 本文将政策改革的福利度量分解为可归因于再分配的部分和由于效率的部分。我们进一步将效率分解为子部分，例如从更好的针对个体特有风险和总体风险的保险中获得的收益。我们对与替代可行分配相关的福利度量进行分解，使用的坐标系统采用广义帕累托-内吉希权重来捕捉不平等，以及生产和消费楔子来捕捉扭曲。我们的分解具有几个理想属性。它将沿着帕累托前沿的运动引起的福利变化归因于再分配；将负面效率变化归因于远离帕累托前沿的运动；并且产生的福利变化的子部分份额是货币不变和相对于改革方向对称的。我们的分解可以用隐含的税收和转移系统来解释，其中再分配捕捉实际收入变化，效率捕捉无谓损失，产出成本。

### 全球外汇融资中的地缘经济竞争与资本重新分配
**[Geoeconomic Competition and Capital Reallocation in Global FX Funding](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34908)**

**Authors**: Yu An, Amy W. Huber

**Abstract**: We study geoeconomic competition and capital reallocation in global financial markets, using the foreign exchange (FX) funding market as our empirical setting. FX funding, obtained by borrowing one currency while pledging another through FX swaps, is instrumental to cross-border investment and provides high-frequency measures of capital reallocation. Countries compete for FX funding through policy actions that shift investment returns or funding costs, thereby inducing global portfolio rebalancing by private investors. We quantify this competition by measuring how one country’s inflow responds to another country’s actions, which we call “reallocation exposure.” Because observed funding flows reflect common shocks and strategic interactions across countries, bilateral influence is difficult to identify. We resolve this challenge by identifying “funding fronts,” the independent margins of portfolio adjustment that enable systematic estimation of reallocation exposure. Applying our framework to a proprietary dataset, we find that FX funding competition is concentrated in a small number of funding fronts, with a dominant U.S. dollar front accounting for most capital reallocation. Consequently, changes in U.S. conditions generate disproportionately large reallocations elsewhere. We use reallocation exposure to construct time-varying measures of geoeconomic power and show that variations systematically track major monetary, fiscal, and geopolitical events. Finally, we characterize the network of financial competition and cooperation and show that strategic responses implied by reallocation exposure align with cross-country movements in policy rates.

**摘要**: 我们研究了全球金融市场中的地缘经济竞争和资本重新配置，以外汇（FX）融资市场作为我们的实证背景。外汇融资是通过外汇掉期借入一种货币同时抵押另一种货币获得的，对于跨境投资至关重要，并提供了资本重新配置的高频度量。各国通过改变投资回报或融资成本的政策行动来争夺外汇融资，从而引发私人投资者进行全球投资组合的再平衡。我们通过衡量一个国家的流入对另一个国家行动的反应来量化这种竞争，我们称之为“重新配置暴露”。由于观察到的资金流动反映了国家间的共同冲击和战略互动，双边影响难以识别。我们通过识别“融资前线”，即投资组合调整的独立边际，来解决这一挑战，这使我们能够系统地估计重新配置暴露。将我们的框架应用于专有数据集，我们发现外汇融资竞争集中在少数几个融资前线，以美元前线为主导，占大部分资本重新配置。因此，美国条件的变化在其他地方产生了不成比例的巨大重新配置。我们利用重新配置暴露构建了随时间变化的地缘经济力量度量，并表明这些变化系统地追踪了主要的货币、财政和地缘政治事件。最后，我们描述了金融竞争和合作的网络，并表明重新配置暴露所暗示的战略响应与跨国政策利率的变动一致。

### 多维信号与文化政治的兴起
**[Multidimensional Signaling and the Rise of Cultural Politics](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34909)**

**Authors**: Daron Acemoglu, Georgy Egorov, Konstantin Sonin

**Abstract**: In turbulent times, political labels become increasingly uninformative about politicians’ true policy preferences or their ability to withstand the influence of special interest groups. We offer a model in which politicians use campaign rhetoric to signal their political preferences in multiple dimensions. In equilibrium, the less popular types try to pool with the more popular ones, whereas the more popular types seek to separate themselves. The ability of voters to process information shapes politicians’ campaign rhetoric. If the signals on the cultural dimension are more precise, politicians signal more there, even if the economy is more important to voters. The unpopular type benefits from increased conformity, which bridges the candidates’ rhetoric and makes it more difficult for voters to make an informed decision.

**摘要**: 在动荡时期，政治标签越来越不能提供关于政治人物真实政策偏好或他们抵御特殊利益集团影响能力的信息。我们提出了一个模型，在这个模型中，政治人物使用竞选言论来在多个维度上表明他们的政治偏好。在均衡状态下，不那么受欢迎的类型试图与更受欢迎的类型混在一起，而更受欢迎的类型则寻求将自己区分开来。选民处理信息的能力塑造了政治人物的竞选言论。如果文化维度上的信号更精确，即使经济对选民更重要，政治人物也会更多地在那里发出信号。不受欢迎的类型从增加的一致性中受益，这弥合了候选人的言论，使得选民更难做出明智的决定。

### 人工智能、人类认知与知识崩溃
**[AI, Human Cognition and Knowledge Collapse](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34910)**

**Authors**: Daron Acemoglu, Dingwen Kong, Asuman Ozdaglar

**Abstract**: We study how generative AI, and in particular agentic AI, shapes human learning incentives and the long-run evolution of society’s information ecosystem. We build a dynamic model of learning and decision-making in which successful decisions require combining shared, community-level general knowledge with individual-level, context-specific knowledge; these two inputs are complements. Learning exhibits economies of scope: costly human effort jointly produces a private signal about their own context and a “thin” public signal that accumulates into the community’s stock of general knowledge, generating a learning externality. Agentic AI delivers context-specific recommendations that substitute for human effort. By contrast, a richer stock of general knowledge complements human effort by raising its marginal return. The model highlights a sharp dynamic tension: while agentic AI can improve contemporaneous decision quality, it can also erode learning incentives that sustain long-run collective knowledge. When human effort is sufficiently elastic and agentic recommendations exceed an accuracy threshold, the economy can tip into a knowledge-collapse steady state in which general knowledge vanishes ultimately, despite high-quality personalized advice. Welfare is generally non-monotone in agentic accuracy, implying an interior, welfare-maximizing level of agentic precision and motivating information-design regulations. In contrast, greater aggregation capacity for general knowledge—meaning more effective sharing and pooling of human-generated general knowledge—unambiguously raises welfare and increases resilience to knowledge collapse.

**摘要**: 我们研究了生成性人工智能，特别是代理性人工智能，如何塑造人类学习的激励机制以及社会信息生态系统的长期演变。我们构建了一个动态模型，用于学习和决策，成功的决策需要结合共享的社区级一般知识与个体级的、特定情境的知识；这两种输入是互补的。学习表现出范围经济：昂贵的人类努力共同产生一个关于他们自己情境的私人信号和一个“薄”的公共信号，这些信号积累成为社区的一般知识储备，产生学习外部性。代理性人工智能提供特定情境的建议，替代了人类努力。相比之下，更丰富的一般知识储备通过提高其边际回报来补充人类努力。该模型突出了一个明显的动态张力：虽然代理性人工智能可以提高同期决策质量，但它也可能侵蚀维持长期集体知识的学习激励。当人类努力足够弹性，且代理性建议超过一个准确性阈值时，经济可能会陷入一个知识崩溃的稳态，在这种状态下，一般知识最终消失，尽管有高质量的个性化建议。福利通常不是单调的代理性准确性，这意味着存在一个内部的、最大化福利的代理性精度水平，并激励信息设计法规。相比之下，对一般知识的更大聚合能力——意味着更有效的共享和汇集人类产生的一般知识——无疑会提高福利并增加对知识崩溃的抵抗力。

### 利率如何影响消费？家庭债务和资产价格的作用

**Authors**: Angus K. Foulis, Jonathon Hazell, Atif R. Mian, Belinda Tracey

**Abstract**: This paper estimates how rate cuts increase consumption, via debt and asset prices. ### 关税经济学
**[The Economics of Tariffs](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34915)**

**Authors**: Ralph Ossa, Stephen J. Redding

**Abstract**: A central insight from neoclassical economics is that international trade operates like an improvement in production technology. It generates mutual aggregate welfare gains for countries as a whole, but creates winners and losers within countries. Tariffs are a tax on this trading technology and distort the prices faced by domestic consumers and producers. Large countries can use tariffs to improve their terms of trade on world markets. But if all countries try to do so, they can end up with lower welfare than if they cooperated to liberalize trade. Tariffs can be used to redistribute income between the winners and losers from trade within countries. But there can be other more efficient ways to achieve redistribution. Policies to promote economic activity in critical industries can be rationalized based on externalities or national security. But these arguments typically rationalize targeted policies towards those industries and tariffs can be dominated by other policy interventions. Empirical findings from the recent waves of U.S. tariffs suggest that most of the incidence of these tariffs has been borne by U.S. importers, wholesalers, retailers and consumers rather than by foreign exporters. These tariffs have led to a large-scale reorganization of U.S. supply chains away from China to third countries. Although this reorganization has substantially reduced China's share of U.S. imports, the U.S. remains indirectly exposed to China through the imports of these third countries.

**摘要**: 新古典经济学的一个核心观点是，国际贸易就像生产技术的进步。它为各国整体创造了相互的总体福利收益，但也在国家内部创造了赢家和输家。关税是对这种交易技术的征税，扭曲了国内消费者和生产者面临的价格。大国可以通过关税来改善其在世界市场上的贸易条件。但如果所有国家都试图这样做，他们最终可能会比合作自由化贸易时获得更低的福利。关税可以用来在国家内部从贸易中获益和受损的人之间重新分配收入。但可能还有其他更有效的方式来实现再分配。基于外部性或国家安全，可以合理化促进关键行业经济活动的措施。但这些论点通常合理化针对这些行业的针对性政策，而关税可以被其他政策干预所取代。最近几波美国关税的实证研究发现，这些关税的大部分负担是由美国的进口商、批发商、零售商和消费者承担，而不是由外国出口商承担。这些关税导致了美国供应链从中国大规模重组到第三国。尽管这种重组大大减少了中国在美国进口中的份额，但美国仍然通过这些第三国的进口间接暴露于中国。

### 气候灾难与代际公平：可持续发展的财政规则
**[Climate Disasters and Intergenerational Equity: A Fiscal Rule for Sustainable Development](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34916)**

**Authors**: Harrison Hong, Heqing Huang, Neng Wang

**Abstract**: Climate disasters threaten intergenerational equity by exposing future generations to rising risks. We develop a model in which a government learns about disaster risk and enforces a sustainability criterion requiring expected social welfare to be non-decreasing over time. This criterion—similar to the principle underlying the UN Sustainable Development Goals—can be decentralized through state-contingent fiscal instruments: when perceived disaster risk is high, the constraint binds and government raises a consumption tax to finance investment subsidies for resilience. Such a fiscal rule leads to intergenerational-welfare smoothing and improves asset valuations despite adverse climate news due to commitments to future resilience. Compared with a government that adopts a social discount rate lower than households’, the sustainability-constraint rule responds to disaster risk and is better aligned with observed consumption-based climate-resilience taxes, such as those implemented in Greece and Spain.

**摘要**: 气候灾害通过使后代面临日益增长的风险，威胁到代际公平。我们开发了一个模型，其中政府了解灾害风险，并执行一个可持续性标准，要求预期社会福利随时间不减少。这一标准——类似于联合国可持续发展目标背后的原理——可以通过状态依存的财政工具来分散实施：当感知到的灾害风险高时，约束条件生效，政府提高消费税以资助弹性投资补贴。这样的财政规则导致代际福利平滑，并改善了资产估值，尽管气候新闻不利，但由于承诺未来的弹性。与采用低于家庭的社会贴现率的政府相比，可持续性约束规则响应灾害风险，并且与观察到的基于消费的气候弹性税更为一致，例如在希腊和西班牙实施的那些。
