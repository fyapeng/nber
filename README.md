### 智能市场的兴起：大型语言模型的价格、供应和需求
**[The Emerging Market for Intelligence: Pricing, Supply, and Demand for LLMs](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34608)**

**Authors**: Mert Demirer, Andrey Fradkin, Nadav Tadelis, Sida Peng

**Abstract**: We document six facts about the structure and dynamics of the LLM market using API usage data from OpenRouter and Microsoft Azure. First, we show rapid growth in the number of models, creators, and inference providers, driven by open-source entrants. Second, we show price declines and persistent price heterogeneity across and within intelligence tiers, with open-source models being 90% cheaper than comparable closed-source models of the same intelligence. Third, we document market dynamism, with frequent turnover among leading models and creators. Fourth, we present evidence of horizontal and vertical differentiation, with no single model dominating across use cases, and demand for intelligence varying widely across applications. Fifth, we estimate preliminary short-run price elasticities just above one, suggesting limited scope for Jevons-Paradox effects. Finally, we show that although the share of firms that use multiple models increased over time, most firms concentrate their use on a single model, consistent with experimentation rather than persistent reliance on multiple models.

**摘要**: 我们利用来自OpenRouter和微软Azure的API使用数据，记录了LLM（大型语言模型）市场的结构和动态的六个事实。首先，我们展示了模型、创造者和推理提供者数量的快速增长，这一增长是由开源参与者推动的。其次，我们展示了价格下降和跨智能层级内外持续存在价格异质性，开源模型的价格比同等智能的闭源模型便宜90%。第三，我们记录了市场的动态性，领先模型和创造者之间频繁更迭。第四，我们提出了水平和垂直差异化的证据，没有单一模型在用例中占主导地位，不同应用对智能的需求差异很大。第五，我们估计了初步的短期价格弹性略高于一，这表明Jevons悖效（即技术进步导致资源消耗增加）的影响有限。最后，我们展示了尽管使用多种模型的公司份额随时间增加，但大多数公司集中使用单一模型，这与实验性使用而非持续依赖多种模型一致。

### 人力资本与发展
**[Human Capital and Development](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34602)**

**Authors**: Philippe Aghion, Ingvild Almås, Costas Meghir

**Abstract**: Human capital is central to efforts to promote growth, convergence, and the elimination of poverty. Drawing on the seminal macroeconomic frameworks by Nelson-Phelps, Lucas and subsequent developments, alongside macro and microeconomic evidence, we examine the role of human capital in driving innovation and growth. We highlight how different types of human capital, characterized by education level, matter in different stages of development. Despite documented increases in years of schooling, the world’s poorest regions still see stagnating outcomes in learning and education quality, potentially creating poverty traps where investments in neither physical nor human capital materialize. We discuss obstacles to human capital accumulation through a simple analytical framework and present evidence from randomized interventions spanning early childhood programs to school-age initiatives, assessing policies that can effectively remove barriers to skill acquisition and establish foundations for sustained growth.

**摘要**: 人力资本是促进增长、趋同和消除贫困的核心。借鉴纳尔逊-菲尔普斯、卢卡斯的开创性宏观经济框架以及随后的发展，结合宏观和微观经济证据，我们考察了人力资本在推动创新和增长中的作用。我们强调了不同类型人力资本，以教育水平为特征，在不同发展阶段的重要性。尽管记录显示上学年数有所增加，但世界上最贫穷的地区在学习和教育质量方面的成果仍然停滞不前，可能造成贫困陷阱，即对物质资本和人力资本的投资都没有实现。我们通过一个简单的分析框架讨论了人力资本积累的障碍，并从随机干预中提出证据，这些干预涵盖了从儿童早期项目到学龄儿童倡议，评估了可以有效地消除技能获取障碍并为持续增长奠定基础的政策。

### 气候创新的经济学：技术、气候政策与清洁能源转型
**[The Economics of Climate Innovation: Technology, Climate Policy, and the Clean Energy Transition](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34601)**

**Authors**: Eugenie Dugoua, Jacob Moscona

**Abstract**: This chapter examines the economics of climate innovation and its role in the clean technology transition. It outlines the incentives, market failures, and policy levers that shape the development and diffusion of clean technologies; traces global patterns in technology development and deployment; and highlights frontier challenges and open questions related to climate adaptation, critical mineral supply chains, artificial intelligence, and geopolitics. The analysis explores the role of effective climate policy, stressing the relevance of coordinated approaches that match instruments to technology maturity and local context.

**摘要**: 本章考察了气候创新的经济学及其在清洁技术转型中的作用。它概述了激励措施、市场失灵和政策杠杆，这些因素塑造了清洁技术的发展和传播；追踪了全球技术发展和部署的模式；并强调了与气候适应、关键矿产供应链、人工智能和地缘政治相关的前沿挑战和未决问题。分析探讨了有效的气候政策的作用，强调了协调方法的相关性，这些方法将工具与技术成熟度和当地环境相匹配。

### 开放设备和切片：来自Wi-Fi设备的证据
**[Open Devices and Slices: Evidence From Wi-Fi Equipment](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34603)**

**Authors**: Do Yoon Kim, Roberto Fontana, Shane Greenstein

**Abstract**: The study examines the quasi-natural experiments provided by the staggered introduction of open drivers in the supply chains for routers. It is rare to observe components become open and measure whether openness generates a statistical impact on more products and innovative products. This study collects novel data on all routers and subcomponents introduced between 2000 and 2018, characterizing each firm's position in a supply chain as either an upstream component provider or a downstream router assembler. Following prior literature, openness influences a firm's ability to negotiate with current and potential partners, which is labeled as autonomy. Evidence suggests that openness enhances supplier autonomy, increases the introduction of new products, and leads to a greater number of products located closer to the technical frontier. These estimates suggest that openness increased product introductions by enlarging the options available to component suppliers. The largest component suppliers benefited from greater sales, and no evidence indicates that openness aided entrants, small firms, or assemblers.

**摘要**: 该研究考察了在路由器供应链中逐步引入开放驱动程序所提供的准自然实验。很少有机会观察到组件变得开放，并衡量开放性是否对更多产品和创新产品产生了统计影响。本研究收集了2000年至2018年间所有路由器和子组件的新颖数据，将每个公司在供应链中的位置特征化为上游组件提供商或下游路由器组装商。遵循先前文献，开放性影响公司与当前和潜在合作伙伴进行谈判的能力，这被称为自主性。证据表明，开放性增强了供应商的自主性，增加了新产品的推出，并导致更多产品接近技术前沿。这些估计表明，开放性通过扩大组件供应商的可选范围增加了产品推出。最大的组件供应商从更大的销售额中受益，没有证据表明开放性帮助了新进入者、小公司或组装商。

### 联系更好还是更糟？生产网络在金融危机中的作用
**[Connected for Better or Worse? The Role of Production Networks in Financial Crises](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34604)**

**Authors**: Jorge Miranda-Pinto, Eugenio I. Rojas, Felipe Saffie, Alvaro Silva

**Abstract**: We study how production networks shape the severity of Sudden Stops. We build a small open economy model with collateral constraints and input–output linkages, derive a sufficient statistic that maps network structure into the amplification of tradable shocks, and show that a planner optimally introduces sectoral wedges to reduce amplification. Using OECD input–output data and Sudden Stop episodes, we document systematic network differences between emerging and advanced economies and show they predict crisis severity. A calibrated three-sector DSGE model disciplined by these differences reveals that endowing an advanced economy with an emerging-market production network moves most of the way toward the observed emerging–advanced Sudden Stop gap.

**摘要**: 我们研究了生产网络如何影响“突然停止”的严重性。我们构建了一个具有抵押约束和投入产出联系的小开放经济模型，推导出一个充分统计量，将网络结构映射到可贸易冲击的放大效应，并展示规划者会最优地引入部门楔子以减少放大效应。利用经合组织投入产出数据和“突然停止”事件，我们记录了新兴经济体和先进经济体之间系统性的网络差异，并展示它们预测了危机的严重性。一个校准过的三部门DSGE模型，受到这些差异的约束，揭示了将先进经济体赋予新兴市场生产网络，将大部分解释观察到的新兴-先进“突然停止”差距。

### 家庭中的女性力量
**[Women's Power in the Household](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34605)**

**Authors**: Seema Jayachandran, Alessandra Voena

**Abstract**: We examine women's household power in low- and middle-income countries, synthesizing theoretical frameworks and empirical evidence on its measurement, determinants, and consequences. We define women's household power as their influence over household choices, distinguishing it from broader empowerment concepts. We review economic models, including unitary, collective, and bargaining frameworks, and map these to empirical approaches. We then discuss measurement methods  such as structural estimation of consumption allocation, survey measures, and laboratory experiments. On the determinants of women's power, we find that some approaches, such as transfers targeted to women, show mixed results, while others, such as increasing women's control over their earnings, show clearer positive impacts. On the effects of women's power, we pay special attention to children's human capital. Few studies provide strong evidence that mothers invest more in children than fathers do, but collectively the evidence suggests such an effect. We conclude by highlighting research and methodological gaps.

**摘要**: 我们研究了低收入和中等收入国家女性的家务权力，综合了关于其测量、决定因素和后果的理论框架和实证证据。我们将女性的家务权力定义为她们对家庭选择的影响力，并将其与更广泛的赋权概念区分开来。我们回顾了经济模型，包括单元性、集体性和讨价还价框架，并将这些模型与实证方法对应起来。然后我们讨论了测量方法，如消费分配的结构性估计、调查测量和实验室实验。在女性权力的决定因素方面，我们发现一些方法，如针对女性的转移支付，结果不一，而其他方法，如增加女性对其收入的控制，显示出更清晰的积极影响。在女性权力的影响方面，我们特别关注儿童的人力资本。很少有研究提供强有力的证据表明母亲比父亲在孩子身上投资更多，但总体而言，证据表明存在这样的效应。我们最后强调了研究和方法上的差距。

### 医疗创新的长期经济效应及其机遇的作用
**[The Long Run Economic Effects of Medical Innovation and the Role of Opportunities](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34606)**

**Authors**: Sonia R. Bhalotra, Damian Clarke, Atheendar Venkataramani

**Abstract**: We leverage the introduction of the first antibiotic therapies in 1937 to examine the long-run effects of early-childhood pneumonia on adult educational attainment, employment, income, and work-related disability. Using census data, we document large average improvements across all outcomes, alongside substantial heterogeneity by gender and race. Among women, health gains led to changes in marriage and fertility that partially offset their labor market improvements. Among Black Americans, we uncover a pronounced gradient linked to systemic racial discrimination in the pre–Civil Rights era: individuals born in more discriminatory Jim Crow states realized much smaller gains than those born in less discriminatory states, despite larger reductions in pneumonia exposure. There is no similar gradient among white Americans. Together, these findings highlight the central role of institutional environments in shaping whether investments in early-life health translate into long-run socioeconomic gains.

**摘要**: 我们利用1937年首批抗生素疗法的引入来研究儿童早期肺炎对成人教育程度、就业、收入和工作相关残疾的长期影响。利用人口普查数据，我们记录了所有结果的平均大幅改善，以及按性别和种族的显著异质性。在女性中，健康收益导致了婚姻和生育的变化，部分抵消了她们在劳动市场的改善。在非裔美国人中，我们发现了一个与民权时代前系统性种族歧视密切相关的明显梯度：在更具歧视性的吉姆·克劳州出生的个体比在较不歧视的州出生的个体实现了更小的收益，尽管肺炎暴露的减少更大。在白人美国人中没有类似的梯度。这些发现共同强调了制度环境在塑造早期生活中健康投资是否转化为长期社会经济收益中的核心作用。

### 公共交通对家庭的好处：来自印度的证据
**[The Benefits of Public Transit to Households: Evidence from India](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34613)**

**Authors**: Palak Suri, Maureen L. Cropper

**Abstract**: We measure benefits to households from Mumbai’s new Metro rail system. We estimate a commute mode choice model to value commute time savings in the short run and a housing choice model to value the improved commuting utility that households experience due to spatial sorting. Aggregate benefits from Metro rail are over 10 times higher when spatial sorting occurs. In the short run women, college-educated workers, and workers with above median incomes experience higher benefits than their opposites. In the long run, households with lower incomes and assets and less than college education benefit more than their wealthier counterparts.

**摘要**: 我们衡量了孟买新地铁系统对家庭的好处。我们估计了一个通勤方式选择模型，以评估短期内通勤时间节省的价值，并估计了一个住房选择模型，以评估由于空间排序，家庭因改善通勤效用而获得的好处。当空间排序发生时，地铁的总收益超过10倍。在短期内，女性、受过大学教育的工人和收入高于中位数的工人比他们的对立面获得更高的收益。从长远来看，收入和资产较低且教育程度低于大学的家庭比更富有的同行获得更多的收益。

### 配偶社会保障索赔决策对寡妇财务冲击的影响
**[The Impact of Spousal Social Security Claiming Decisions on the Financial Shock of Widowhood](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34612)**

**Authors**: Sita Slavov

**Abstract**: This paper presents evidence suggesting that delayed Social Security claiming by husbands – resulting in an actuarially enhanced benefit – attenuates the financial shock of widowhood for their wives. Under Social Security survivor benefit rules, primary earners (usually husbands) pass on the actuarial adjustments from delayed claiming to their surviving spouses. Using a staggered difference-in-differences approach, I find women whose husbands delayed claiming to full retirement age or later face a post-widowhood increase of 6.9 percentage points in the probability of falling below the 5th percentile of the pre-widowhood income distribution. This effect is almost 12 percent smaller for each year of delayed claiming by the husband (though the attenuation is concentrated in the first 4 years of widowhood). The general findings are robust to instrumenting for the husband’s claiming age using the loosening of the retirement earnings test in 2000 – a policy change that incentivized earlier claiming.

**摘要**: 本文提供了证据，表明丈夫延迟申领社会保障金——导致精算增强型福利——减轻了他们妻子丧偶后的财务冲击。根据社会保障遗属福利规则，主要收入者（通常是丈夫）将延迟申领的精算调整传递给他们的遗孀。使用分阶段的差异-差异方法，我发现那些丈夫延迟申领至完全退休年龄或更晚的女性，在丧偶后低于丧偶前收入分布的第5百分位的概率增加了6.9个百分点。这种效应几乎每延迟一年申领就减少12%（尽管这种减轻集中在丧偶后的前4年）。总体发现对于使用2000年放宽退休收入测试的政策变化——这一政策变化鼓励了更早的申领——作为丈夫申领年龄的工具是稳健的。

### 具有家庭和企业异质性的新凯恩斯经济学
**[New Keynesian Economics with Household and Firm Heterogeneity](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34611)**

**Authors**: Thomas Winberry, Adrien Auclert, Matthew Rognlie, Ludwig Straub

**Abstract**: The Heterogeneous-Agent New Keynesian literature has revisited the transmission of monetary and fiscal policy to consumption using models where heterogeneous households face idiosyncratic income risk and borrowing constraints. We show that the key lessons from this literature also apply to investment using a model where heterogeneous firms face idiosyncratic productivity risk and financial frictions: constrained firms’ investment depends on their free cash flow, generating indirect effects of monetary policy and implying that transfer payments stimulate investment demand. Quantitatively, the strength of these new mechanisms is governed by firms’ marginal propensities to invest (MPIs), similar to the role of marginal propensities to consume (MPCs) for households. But unlike MPCs, we currently lack quasi-experimental evidence about MPIs that we can use to directly discipline the new mechanisms.

**摘要**: 异质性代理新凯恩斯主义文献重新审视了货币和财政政策对消费的传导，使用的模型中异质性家庭面临特有收入风险和借款限制。我们展示，这些文献中的关键教训也适用于投资，使用的模型中异质性公司面临特有生产率风险和金融摩擦：受限公司的投资取决于它们的自由现金流，产生货币政策的间接效应，并意味着转移支付刺激投资需求。从数量上看，这些新机制的强度由公司的边际投资倾向（MPIs）决定，类似于家庭的边际消费倾向（MPCs）的作用。但与MPCs不同，我们目前缺乏关于MPIs的准实验证据，我们可以使用这些证据直接规范新机制。
