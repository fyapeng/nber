### 关税作为财政政策
**[Tariffs as Fiscal Policy](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34192)**

**Authors**: Kimberly A. Clausing, Maurice Obstfeld

**Abstract**: The year 2025 brought a remarkable shift in the role of tariffs in the US economy, as the Trump administration simultaneously escalated the use of broad tariffs and ensured that Congress enacted large income tax cuts. This fiscal switch has important implications for the US tax system. While maintaining tariff rates at summer 2025 levels would generate large government revenues, such broad tariffs have significant downsides: Efficiency losses would approach one-third of revenues raised, the tax system would be less progressive, and there would be serious tax administration concerns. The fiscal shift also has significant macroeconomic implications, although probably not the intended ones. Broad tariffs generate a large negative supply shock, simultaneously raising prices and reducing macroeconomic activity.

**摘要**: 2025年，美国经济中关税的作用发生了显著变化，因为特朗普政府一方面加大了广泛关税的使用，另一方面确保国会通过了大规模的所得税减免。这一财政转变对美国税制有着重要的影响。尽管将关税率维持在2025年夏季的水平将产生大量的政府收入，但这种广泛的关税有其显著的负面效应：效率损失接近所筹集收入的三分之一，税制将变得不那么累进，并且将引发严重的税务管理问题。财政转变还具有重要的宏观经济影响，尽管可能并非预期的那些。广泛的关税产生了巨大的负面供应冲击，同时提高价格并减少宏观经济活动。

### 私募股权、消费者和竞争：来自养老院行业的证据
**[Private Equity, Consumers, and Competition: Evidence from the Nursing Home Industry](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34306)**

**Authors**: Ashvin Gandhi, YoungJun Song, Prabhava Upadrashta

**Abstract**: This paper studies how product market competition shapes the impact of private equity (PE) acquisitions on consumers. We examine nursing home buyouts and observe that PE-owned facilities exhibit greater competitive sensitivity: competing more aggressively when competitive incentives are strong and exploiting market power more aggressively when competitive incentives are weak. We find that PE-owned facilities are more sensitive to local market competition—even when comparing effects only across facilities purchased as part of the same acquisition—and are more responsive to a pro-competitive policy helping consumers compare facilities. This suggests that the competitive sensitivity of acquirers and the concentration of markets where acquisitions occur are important factors contributing to the effects of a merger, as well as that pro-competitive polices can reshape the effects of PE ownership on consumers.

**摘要**: 本文研究了产品市场竞争如何影响私募股权（PE）收购对消费者的影响。我们检验了养老院的收购情况，并观察到PE拥有的设施表现出更大的竞争敏感性：在竞争激励强烈时更加积极地竞争，在竞争激励较弱时更加积极地利用市场力量。我们发现PE拥有的设施对当地市场竞争更为敏感——即使仅在作为同一次收购一部分购买的设施之间比较效果——并且对一项有助于消费者比较设施的亲竞争政策更为响应。这表明，收购者的竞争敏感性和收购发生的市场集中度是影响合并效果的重要因素，同时亲竞争政策可以重塑PE所有权对消费者的影响。

### 需要什么？量化欧元区内的跨国转移
**[What Does It Take? Quantifying Cross-Country Transfers in the Eurozone](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34311)**

**Authors**: Yi-Li Chien, Zhengyang Jiang, Matteo Leombroni, Hanno Lustig

**Abstract**: We compute the cross-country transfers that result from unconventional monetary policy in the Eurozone. The ECB funds the expansion of its aggregate balance sheet mostly by issuing bank reserves and cash in core countries. The national central banks (NCBs) in periphery countries then borrow from the core NCBs at below-market rates to fund the asset purchases and bank lending. In addition, NCBs in the periphery lend more to their own banks at below-market rates. To compute the cross-country transfers, we compare the resulting cross-country distribution of NCB income to a counterfactual scenario without the ECB and without non-marketable intra-Eurozone debt. We document significant and persistent transfers from the core to the periphery.

**摘要**: 我们计算了欧元区非常规货币政策导致的跨国转移。欧洲央行主要通过发行银行储备和核心国家的现金来为其总体资产负债表的扩张提供资金。然后，外围国家的国家中央银行（NCBs）以低于市场利率向核心国家的NCBs借款，以资助资产购买和银行贷款。此外，外围国家的NCBs也以低于市场利率向自己的银行提供更多贷款。为了计算跨国转移，我们将NCB收入的跨国分布与没有欧洲央行和没有不可市场化的欧元区内债务的反事实情景进行比较。我们记录了从核心到外围的重大且持续的转移。

### 人工智能在研究与开发中的应用
**[Artificial Intelligence in Research and Development](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34312)**

**Authors**: Benjamin Jones

**Abstract**: How much can AI accelerate progress in different research fields?  This paper shows that three features—the share of research tasks AI performs, the productivity of AI at those tasks, and the strength of bottlenecks—are key determinants of AI’s implications in any area, from cancer therapeutics to software design. The model maps changes in AI capabilities to research outcomes, quantifies the “marginal returns to intelligence,” and shows how AI can shift returns to R&D investment. Concepts like superintelligence, Powerful AI, and Transformative AI are further engaged and disciplined.  Finally, the framework sets a measurement agenda linking AI benchmarks to field-specific opportunities for accelerating progress.

**摘要**: 人工智能能在多大程度上加速不同研究领域的进展？这篇论文表明，三个特征——人工智能执行的研究任务份额、人工智能在这些任务中的生产力，以及瓶颈的强度——是决定人工智能在任何领域影响的关键因素，从癌症治疗到软件设计。该模型将人工智能能力的变化映射到研究结果，量化了“智能的边际回报”，并展示了人工智能如何转移研发投资的回报。超智能、强大的人工智能和变革性人工智能等概念得到了进一步的参与和规范。最后，该框架设定了一个测量议程，将人工智能基准与特定领域的加速进展机会联系起来。

### 理解和应对温度对死亡率的影响
**[Understanding and Addressing Temperature Impacts on Mortality](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34313)**

**Authors**: Marshall Burke, Andrew J. Wilson, Tumenkhusel Avirmed, Jonas Wallstein, Mariana C. M. Martins, Patrick Behrer, Christopher W. Callahan, Marissa Childs, June Choi, Karina French, Carlos F. Gould, Sam Heft-Neal, Renzhi Jing, Minghao Qiu, Lisa Rennels, Emma Krasovich Southworth

**Abstract**: A large literature documents how ambient temperature affects human mortality. Using decades of detailed data from 30 countries, we revisit and synthesize key findings from this literature. We confirm that ambient temperature is among the largest external threats to human health, and is responsible for a remarkable 5-12% of total deaths across countries in our sample, or hundreds of thousands of deaths per year in both the U.S. and EU. In all contexts we consider, cold kills more than heat, though the temperature of minimum risk rises with age, making younger individuals more vulnerable to heat and older individuals more vulnerable to cold. We find evidence for adaptation to the local climate, with hotter places experiencing somewhat lower risk at higher temperatures, but still more overall mortality from heat due to more frequent exposure. Within countries, higher income is not associated with uniformly lower vulnerability to ambient temperature, and the overall burden of mortality from ambient temperature is not falling over time. Finally, we systematically summarize the limited set of studies that rigorously evaluate interventions that can reduce the impact of heat and cold on health. We find that many proposed and implemented policy interventions lack empirical support and do not target temperature exposures that generate the highest health burden, and that some of the most beneficial interventions for reducing the health impacts of cold or heat have little explicit to do with climate.

**摘要**: 大量文献记录了环境温度如何影响人类死亡率。利用来自30个国家数十年的详细数据，我们重新审视并综合了这一文献的关键发现。我们确认环境温度是人类健康的最大的外部威胁之一，在我们的样本国家中，它导致了令人瞩目的5-12%的总死亡，或每年在美国和欧盟造成数十万人死亡。在我们考虑的所有情况下，寒冷造成的死亡比热浪要多，尽管最小风险的温度随着年龄的增长而上升，使得年轻人更容易受到热浪的影响，而老年人更容易受到寒冷的影响。我们发现有证据表明对当地气候的适应，较热的地方在较高温度下经历的风险略低，但由于更频繁的暴露，总体上因热浪导致的死亡更多。在国家内部，较高的收入并不与对环境温度的较低脆弱性一致相关，且由环境温度引起的死亡负担总体上并未随时间下降。最后，我们系统总结了有限的研究，这些研究严格评估了可以减少热浪和寒冷对健康影响的干预措施。我们发现许多提出和实施的政策干预措施缺乏经验支持，并未针对产生最大健康负担的温度暴露，一些最有益的减少寒冷或热浪对健康影响的干预措施与气候几乎没有直接关系。

### 将非单位收入弹性、窒息价格和窒息收入纳入应用一般均衡模型
**[Incorporating Non-Unitary Income Elasticities, Choke Prices and Choke Incomes into Applied General-Equilibrium Models](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34314)**

**Authors**: James R. Markusen

**Abstract**: Traditional applied general-equilibrium (AGE) models have always faced trade-offs between analytical and computational tractability and counter-empirical restrictions. One is the assumption of homothetic preferences implying unitary income elasticities of demand, significantly inconsistent with data. Similarly, there is no “choke” income level, below which a certain good is not purchased and there is no choke price above which a good is not purchased, implying no changes in the extensive margin of trade. Here I exploit what I will label a Stone-Geary Modified (SGM) formulation. This produces a model in which there are non-unitary income elasticities, choke income levels for some/all goods, and choke prices. The second approach modifies CRIE (constant relative income elasticity) preferences which are preferred for modeling income elasticities, but don’t by themselves permit choke income and prices. While other authors have explored these properties in alternative ways, both my approaches have considerable advantages for high-dimension simulation models in that they retain CES structures and functional forms so that they can slot right into existing modeling formats. They require only small modifications to off-the-shelf cost and expenditure functions, and therefore goods and factor demand functions via Shepard’s lemma. Unobserved parameters can be calibrated from observed data and econometric estimates.

**摘要**: 传统的应用一般均衡（AGE）模型一直面临着分析和计算可行性与反经验限制之间的权衡。其中之一是假设同质偏好，意味着需求的收入弹性为单位性，这与数据严重不一致。同样，没有“窒息”收入水平，低于此水平时不会购买某种商品，也没有窒息价格，高于此价格时不会购买商品，意味着贸易的广泛边际没有变化。在这里，我利用我将称之为斯通-乔治修改（SGM）公式。这产生了一个模型，其中存在非单位性收入弹性，某些/所有商品的窒息收入水平，以及窒息价格。第二种方法修改了CRIE（恒定相对收入弹性）偏好，这些偏好更适合于模拟收入弹性，但本身不允许窒息收入和价格。虽然其他作者已经以其他方式探索了这些属性，但我的两种方法对于高维模拟模型都有很大的优势，因为它们保留了CES结构和函数形式，因此可以直接插入现有的建模格式。它们只需要对现成的成本和支出函数进行小的修改，因此通过谢泼德引理，商品和要素需求函数也是如此。未观察到的参数可以从观察到的数据和计量经济学估计中进行校准。

### 卫星图像和机器学习可以测量什么？
**[What Can Satellite Imagery and Machine Learning Measure?](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34315)**

**Authors**: Jonathan Proctor, Tamma Carleton, Trinetta Chong, Taryn Fransen, Simon Greenhill, Jessica Katz, Hikari Murayama, Luke Sherman, Jeanette Tseng, Hannah Druckenmiller, Solomon Hsiang

**Abstract**: Satellite imagery and machine learning (SIML) are increasingly being combined to remotely measure social and environmental outcomes, yet use of this technology has been limited by insufficient understanding of its strengths and weaknesses. Here, we undertake the most extensive effort yet to characterize the potential and limits of using a SIML technology to measure ground conditions. We conduct 115 standardized large-scale experiments using a composite high-resolution optical image of Earth and a generalizable SIML technology to evaluate what can be accurately measured and where this technology struggles. We find that SIML alone predicts roughly half the variation in ground measurements on average, and that variables describing human society (e.g. female literacy, R²=0.55) are generally as easily measured as natural variables (e.g. bird diversity, R²=0.55). Patterns of performance across measured variable type, space, income and population density indicate that SIML can likely support many new applications and decision-making use cases, although within quantifiable limits.

**摘要**: 卫星图像和机器学习（SIML）越来越多地被结合起来，以远程测量社会和环境结果，但对这项技术的使用一直受到对其优缺点了解不足的限制。在这里，我们进行了迄今为止最广泛的努力，以描述使用SIML技术测量地面条件的潜力和局限性。我们使用地球的复合高分辨率光学图像和可泛化的SIML技术，进行了115项标准化的大规模实验，以评估可以准确测量的内容以及这项技术在何处挣扎。我们发现，SIML单独平均可以预测地面测量变化的大约一半，描述人类社会（例如女性识字率，R²=0.55）的变量通常和自然变量（例如鸟类多样性，R²=0.55）一样容易被测量。在测量变量类型、空间、收入和人口密度方面的性能模式表明，SIML很可能支持许多新的应用和决策使用案例，尽管这是在可量化的限制之内。

### 按需天才：变革性人工智能的价值
**[Genius on Demand: The Value of Transformative Artificial Intelligence](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34316)**

**Authors**: Ajay K. Agrawal, Joshua S. Gans, Avi Goldfarb

**Abstract**: This paper examines how the emergence of transformative AI systems providing ``genius on demand" would affect knowledge worker allocation and labour market outcomes. We develop a simple model distinguishing between routine knowledge workers, who can only apply existing knowledge with some uncertainty, and genius workers, who create new knowledge at a cost increasing with distance from a known point. When genius capacity is scarce, we find it should be allocated primarily to questions at domain boundaries rather than at midpoints between known answers. **摘要**: 衡量自然的金融价值是困难的，这常常导致用于自然保护和恢复的资金不足。随着发展和气候变化导致沿海风险增加，自然的有形益处之一是它提供的防风暴损害的保护。来自风险行业和其他领域的许多研究评估了湿地在减少风暴期间损害的直接影响。然而，湿地对沿海保护的价值可能扩展到许多其他益处，包括在风暴常见地区的房价。我们使用了来自Zillow的房产级别的住房交易数据，并展示了接近佛罗里达州红树林的地区对房价下跌和分散的调节作用，这些是在主要飓风之后发生的。这些影响在规模上是巨大的，减少了房价损失四分之一或更多的可能2-7个百分点，这相当于对于一栋百万美元的房产来说，条件是飓风发生，价值在2万到4万美元之间。

### 在差异中的差异模型中聚合对处理组的平均处理效应
**[Aggregating Average Treatment Effects on the Treated in Difference-in-Differences Models](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34331)**

**Authors**: Partha Deb, Edward C. Norton, Jeffrey M. Wooldridge, Jeffrey E. Zabel

**Abstract**: For difference-in-differences methods, there has been great attention to obtaining consistent estimates of treatment effects, especially when the treatment effects are heterogeneous. However, there has been little discussion of the importance of weights in aggregating those treatment effects into an overall average treatment effect on the treated. There are many possible ways to aggregate estimated cohort-time treatment effects. We show that the standard software used to estimate Callaway and Sant’Anna’s method uses weights that are not just the number of treated observations in treated years. Instead, the software uses weights that include the number of observations in the reference pre-period instead of only the number of observations in the treated periods. We discuss why the aggregation weights matter and under what circumstances the weights make the most difference.

**摘要**: 对于差异中的差异方法，人们非常关注获得一致的处理效应估计，特别是当处理效应是异质的时候。然而，对于在聚合这些处理效应以得出总体受治疗者的平均处理效应时权重的重要性，讨论却相对较少。聚合估计的队列-时间处理效应有许多可能的方法。我们展示，用于估计卡拉韦和桑塔安娜方法的标准软件使用的权重不仅仅是受治疗年份中受治疗观察值的数量。相反，软件使用的权重包括了参考前期的观察值数量，而不仅仅是受治疗期间的观察值数量。我们讨论了聚合权重为何重要，以及在何种情况下权重的差异最为显著。

### 贸易战与美元锚
**[Trade War and the Dollar Anchor](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34332)**

**Authors**: Tarek Alexander Hassan, Thomas M. Mertens, Jingye Wang, Tony Zhang

**Abstract**: We develop a general-equilibrium model in which the safety of a country's currency and the choice of its exchange-rate regime arise endogenously. Calibrated to pre-2025 data, the model replicates the U.S. dollar’s safety premium, low Treasury yields, and its status as the world's anchor currency. Introducing a trade war that isolates U.S. goods markets from the world erodes the U.S. dollar's safety premium, raises U.S. interest rates, and lowers the world market value of U.S. firms. For sufficiently high tariffs, small economies optimally re-peg to the euro, precipitating a phase shift to a euro-centric international monetary system and a global welfare loss. The analysis implies that persistent trade wars may threaten the financial privileges the United States derives from the dollar’s international role.

**摘要**: 我们开发了一个一般均衡模型，在该模型中，一个国家货币的安全性及其汇率制度的选择是内生产生的。该模型根据2025年之前的数据显示，复制了美元的安全溢价、低国债收益率以及其作为世界锚货币的地位。引入一场将美国商品市场与世界隔离的贸易战，侵蚀了美元的安全溢价，提高了美国的利率，并降低了美国公司的世界市场价值。对于足够高的关税，小国最优地重新挂钩欧元，引发了向以欧元为中心的国际货币体系的相位转变，并造成了全球福利损失。分析表明，持续的贸易战可能会威胁到美国从美元的国际角色中获得的金融特权。
