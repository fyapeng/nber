### 抵押贷款定价中的市场力量：推荐贷款的作用
**[Market Power in Mortgage Pricing: the Role of Referral Lending](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35015)**

**Authors**: Dayin Zhang, Panle Jia Barwick, Lu Han, Jonathan Kroah

**Abstract**: Despite intense competition among mortgage lenders, borrowers face substantial price dispersion. We argue that realtor–loan officer referral networks are a key source of lender market power: by steering homebuyers toward a small set of loan officers, these networks restrict effective borrower choice even in competitive markets. Using a novel dataset linking 81,306 realtors to 102,860 loan officers across 41 states, we document that such networks are pervasive and highly concentrated — 85% of realtors direct over 40% of their clients to fewer than four loan officers — and that this concentration persists and even increases in markets with more lenders. IV estimates indicate that borrowers using referred loan officers pay 18.6 basis points higher mortgage rates, equivalent to $2,609 in upfront costs on the average loan of $306K. Referral lending raises rate spreads by 36.5% and accounts for half of the residual cross-sectional variation in spreads. The premium is nearly three times as large for Hispanic borrowers as for White borrowers, and is systematically higher for Black borrowers and financially constrained households. We identify two channels: referrals reduce borrowers' search intensity across lenders, and referred loan officers exercise pricing power relative to colleagues within the same institution. Efficiency gains from faster processing and reduced denial risk do not offset the additional costs.

**摘要**: 尽管抵押贷款机构之间的竞争非常激烈，但借款人面临的价格差异仍然很大。我们认为，房地产经纪人和贷款官员的推荐网络是贷款机构市场力量的一个关键来源：通过将购房者引导至一小部分贷款官员，这些网络即使在竞争激烈的市场中也限制了借款人的有效选择。利用一个新颖的数据集，我们将81,306名房地产经纪人与41个州的102,860名贷款官员联系起来，记录了这些网络的普遍性和高度集中——85%的房地产经纪人将超过40%的客户推荐给不到四名贷款官员——并且这种集中现象在贷款机构更多的市场中持续存在，甚至有所增加。工具变量估计表明，使用推荐贷款官员的借款人支付的抵押贷款利率高出18.6个基点，相当于平均30.6万美元贷款的前期成本高出2609美元。推荐贷款使利率差扩大了36.5%，并占到了剩余横截面利率差变异的一半。对于西班牙裔借款人来说，这一溢价几乎是白人借款人的三倍，对于黑人借款人和财务受限家庭来说，这一溢价系统性地更高。我们确定了两个渠道：推荐减少了借款人在不同贷款机构中的搜索强度，而被推荐的贷款官员相对于同一机构内的同事行使定价权。更快的处理和降低拒贷风险的效率增益并没有抵消额外的成本。

### 西班牙退休人员健康不平等的趋势
**[Trends in Health Inequalities among Spanish Retirees](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35105)**

**Authors**: Cristina Bellés-Obrero, Manuel Flores Mallo, Pilar García-Gómez, Sergi Jimenez-Martin, Judit Vall Castelló

**Abstract**: Spain, with one of the highest life expectancies globally and a rapidly ageing population, faces growing challenges in sustaining its pension, healthcare, and long-term care systems. This study examines trends in health inequalities among retired Spaniards from 2004 to 2022, using eight waves of the Survey of Health, Ageing and Retirement in Europe (SHARE). We analyse five health outcomes—limitations in daily and instrumental activities, number of chronic conditions, a composite health deficiency index, mental health (EURO-D scale), and cognitive performance—and use linear regression to assess income-related gradients, adjusted for age and sex. We also compute a catch-up time measure—the number of years a poorer individual would need to reach the same level of health as a richer individual—and concentration indices of bad health. We then examine how these inequalities change over time, allowing us to explore the potential influence of pension reforms within the context of Spain’s Beveridge-style healthcare system and tax-funded long-term care provision. Our results show no clear evidence that health inequality has increased from 2004 to 2022. These findings contribute to understanding how income disparities interact with social protection systems in ageing societies and inform the design of equitable health, long-term care, and pension policies.

**摘要**: 西班牙拥有全球最高的预期寿命之一，并且人口迅速老龄化，面临着维持其养老金、医疗保健和长期护理系统的日益增长的挑战。本研究使用欧洲健康、老龄化和退休调查（SHARE）的八轮数据，检查了2004年至2022年期间退休西班牙人的健康状况不平趋势。我们分析了五个健康结果——日常和工具性活动的限制、慢性疾病数量、综合健康缺陷指数、心理健康（EURO-D量表）和认知表现——并使用线性回归评估收入相关的梯度，同时调整年龄和性别。我们还计算了一个追赶时间度量——一个较贫穷的个体需要多少年才能达到与较富裕个体相同的健康水平——以及不良健康的集中指数。然后，我们检查这些不平等随时间的变化，使我们能够探索养老金改革在西班牙贝弗里奇式医疗保健系统和税收资助的长期护理提供背景下的潜在影响。我们的结果没有明确证据表明，从2004年到2022年健康不平等有所增加。这些发现有助于理解收入差异如何在老龄化社会中与社会保障系统相互作用，并为公平的健康、长期护理和养老金政策的设计提供信息。

### 就业会减缓认知衰退吗？来自劳动市场冲击的证据
**[Does Employment Slow Cognitive Decline? Evidence from Labor Market Shocks](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35117)**

**Authors**: Noah Arman Kouchekinia, David Neumark, Tim A. Bruckner

**Abstract**: With large gains in life expectancy, the population share of disability due to cognitive decline and dementia has substantially increased. Many older adults in the United States leave the workforce well before age 65. Correlational evidence suggests that leaving the workforce before retirement age could accelerate the pace of cognitive decline. We offer causal evidence, using HRS data for the United States, exploiting plausibly exogenous shifts in labor demand in local labor markets as a Bartik instrument for employment variation across these markets. We find substantial declines over time in cognitive scores stemming from negative labor demand shocks. These findings are concentrated among men aged 51 to 64, whose employment decisions and outcomes may be more sensitive to local labor market conditions than are these decisions or outcomes for women or for older men. Our evidence extends past work focusing narrowly on the retirement age window and provides further support to the notion that working to older ages may delay age-related cognitive decline.

**摘要**: 随着预期寿命的大幅增长，因认知能力下降和痴呆症导致的残疾在人口中的比例显著增加。许多美国老年人在65岁之前就离开了劳动力市场。相关证据表明，在退休年龄之前离开劳动力市场可能会加速认知能力的下降。我们提供了因果证据，使用美国的HRS数据，利用当地劳动力市场中劳动需求的合理外生变化作为Bartik工具来解释这些市场中的就业变化。我们发现，由于负面劳动需求冲击，认知得分随时间大幅下降。这些发现集中在51至64岁的男性中，他们的就业决策和结果可能比女性或老年男性更受当地劳动力市场条件的影响。我们的证据扩展了过去仅关注退休年龄窗口的工作，并为工作到更老年龄可能延缓与年龄相关的认知能力下降的观点提供了进一步的支持。

### 理解企业在人工智能方面的努力及其经济影响
**[Understanding Firms' AI Efforts and Their Economic Impact](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35123)**

**Authors**: Tania Babina

**Abstract**: This paper reviews firm-level data on artificial intelligence and the emerging evidence on AI's economic effects. It argues that measurement is central: different AI datasets capture different objects (including invention versus use, internal capability building versus outsourcing, and realized activity versus investor perceptions) and can therefore lead to different conclusions. The paper develops a framework for choosing among these measures and surveys available data sources on firm AI efforts. It synthesizes evidence on AI's effects on firm growth, valuation, productivity, risk, labor, competition, financial markets and applications. The paper concludes by suggesting some ideas for future research.

**摘要**: 本文回顾了关于人工智能的公司层面数据和关于人工智能经济效应的新兴证据。文章认为，测量是核心问题：不同的人工智能数据集捕捉不同的对象（包括发明与使用、内部能力建设与外包，以及实现的活动与投资者感知），因此可能导致不同的结论。本文建立了一个框架，用于在这些测量之间进行选择，并调查了关于公司人工智能努力的可用数据来源。它综合了关于人工智能对公司增长、估值、生产率、风险、劳动力、竞争、金融市场和应用的影响的证据。文章最后提出了一些未来研究的想法。

### 战争的国内政治经济学：来自俄罗斯的证据
**[The Domestic Political Economy of War: Evidence from Russia](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35107)**

**Authors**: Alena Gorbuntsova, Gaurav Khanna, Sultan Mehmood

**Abstract**: Wars are often framed as responses to external threats or shifts in the regional balance of power. Yet they can also serve domestic political ends. This paper studies how Russia’s escalations against Ukraine reshaped support for the regime and redistributed the burdens of war across the population. Combining ethnic Russian shares with election and independent polling data, we exploit two sharp geopolitical shocks, the 2014 annexation of Crimea and the 2022 full-scale invasion, in a difference-in-differences event-study design. We find that provinces with larger ethnic Russian populations exhibit sharp increases in support for President Putin following both episodes. At the same time, battlefield casualties fall disproportionately on regions with lower ethnic Russian shares, and attitudes toward the US and EU deteriorate sharply. On the Ukrainian side, Russian attacks are concentrated in areas with higher ethnic Russian shares rather than in resource-rich provinces. Explanations based on material extraction, Soviet symbolism, or differential exposure to external threats do not account for these patterns. Instead, the evidence is more consistent with ethnic identity playing a central role in the domestic political economy of the war. Our conclusions remain similar in fraud-adjusted electoral outcomes, with alternative ethnicity measures, under bounded departures from parallel trends, and after accounting for several baseline regional differences.

**摘要**: 战争通常被描述为对外部威胁或地区力量平衡变化的反应。然而，它们也可以服务于国内政治目的。本文研究了俄罗斯对乌克兰的升级是如何重塑对政权的支持，并在民众中重新分配战争负担的。我们结合俄罗斯族人口比例、选举和独立民调数据，利用2014年克里米亚吞并和2022年全面入侵这两个尖锐的地缘政治冲击，在差异中的差异事件研究设计中进行分析。我们发现，在这两个事件之后，俄罗斯族人口比例较大的省份对普京总统的支持度急剧上升。与此同时，战场伤亡不成比例地落在俄罗斯族人口比例较低的地区，对美国和欧盟的态度急剧恶化。在乌克兰方面，俄罗斯的攻击集中在俄罗斯族人口比例较高的地区，而不是资源丰富的省份。基于物质提取、苏联象征意义或不同程度暴露于外部威胁的解释并不能解释这些模式。相反，证据更符合种族身份在战争的国内政治经济中扮演核心角色。即使在调整后的选举结果、替代的种族衡量标准、在平行趋势的有界偏差下，以及在考虑了几个基线区域差异之后，我们的结论仍然相似。

### 性别特定的经济冲击与家庭议价能力
**[Gender-Specific Economic Shocks and Household Bargaining Power](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35118)**

**Authors**: Rania Gihleb, Osea Giuntella, Dor Morag

**Abstract**: Using machine learning and product-level data on single men’s and single women’s consumption patterns, we develop an index that quantifies the “gendered” nature of consumer goods.  We use the index to investigate how gender-specific labor market shocks influence spending patterns within heterosexual households. Our findings reveal that industrial robot adoption, which worsened men’s relative economic position, shifted household consumption toward products predominantly purchased by single women. In contrast, the expansion of fracking, which boosted demand for young and less skilled men, yields suggestive but less robust evidence of increased spending on goods favored by single men. Although neither shock significantly altered total spending on children’s products, robot exposure led to a reallocation of spending toward goods more commonly purchased for daughters than for sons.

**摘要**: 利用机器学习和关于单身男性与单身女性消费模式的产品级数据，我们开发了一个指数，用以量化消费品的“性别化”特征。我们利用这个指数来研究性别特定的劳动力市场冲击如何影响异性恋家庭内部的消费模式。我们的发现揭示了工业机器人的采用，这恶化了男性相对于经济地位，使得家庭消费转向了主要由单身女性购买的产品。相比之下，水力压裂的扩张，这增加了对年轻和技能较低男性的需求，提供了暗示性的但不那么有力的证据，表明增加了对单身男性青睐的商品的支出。尽管这两种冲击都没有显著改变儿童产品的总支出，但机器人暴露导致了支出的重新分配，转向了更常为女儿而非儿子购买的商品。

### 保护老年工人的福利效应
**[The Welfare Effects of Protecting Older Workers](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35111)**

**Authors**: Todd Morris, Stefan Staubli, Benoit Dostie

**Abstract**: We evaluate the welfare effects of five provincial mandatory retirement bans in Canada from 2005 to 2009 using linked employer-employee tax data. The bans sharply reduce retirements at age 65, with sizable announcement effects and heterogeneity across industries. Post-65 employment and earnings rise at least 14%, with gains comparable to a two-year increase in pension-eligibility ages. Older workers save more and spouses postpone retirement, benefiting public finances, with no observable effects on mortality or younger workers. Highly exposed firms reduce payroll costs via hiring adjustments while maintaining worker productivity and profitability. Our results suggest that protecting older workers was welfare-improving.

**摘要**: 我们利用加拿大2005年至2009年五项省级强制性退休禁令期间的雇主-雇员税收数据链，评估了这些禁令的福利效应。这些禁令显著降低了65岁的退休人数，具有显著的公告效应和跨行业的异质性。65岁以后的就业和收入至少增加了14%，这一增长与退休金资格年龄增加两年相当。老年工人储蓄更多，配偶推迟退休，这对公共财政有益，对死亡率或年轻工人没有可观察到的影响。高度受影响的公司通过招聘调整降低工资成本，同时保持工人的生产力和盈利能力。我们的结果表明，保护老年工人是提高福利的。

### 改革是如何发生的
**[How Reform Happens](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35119)**

**Authors**: Simeon Djankov, Edward L. Glaeser, Andrei Shleifer

**Abstract**: What determines whether and how regulations are reformed? We use a newly constructed data set of 3,590 successful and failed regulatory reforms in 189 countries, between 2005 and 2022, to address this question. We document that regulations have become more business friendly in some regulatory domains but not others. We also show that regulations are more business friendly in richer than in poorer countries, and that holding initial regulatory levels constant, richer countries also reform more. We present a model in which the successful passage of reforms is shaped by the number of veto points in the approval process, the social returns to reform, and the cost of compensating losers from reform, and then test it using our new data set. We find that richer countries have both higher reform attempt and success rates, but less impact of individual reforms on regulation than poorer countries. These findings are consistent with the model if richer countries are better at reform, perhaps because they can compensate losers more efficiently. Across the world, reform attempt rates are strongly correlated with reform success rates but not with reform impact levels. Within countries, a higher share of technological reform attempts is successful, compared to administrative or legal reforms, consistent with the importance of veto points.

**摘要**: 是什么决定监管改革是否发生以及如何进行改革？我们使用了一个新的数据集，该数据集包含了2005年至2022年间189个国家中3590项成功和失败的监管改革，来解答这个问题。我们记录了在某些监管领域，监管变得更加对商业友好，但在其他领域则没有。我们还展示了在较富裕的国家，监管比在较贫穷的国家更对商业友好，并且在保持初始监管水平不变的情况下，较富裕的国家也进行了更多的改革。我们提出了一个模型，其中改革的成功通过受到批准过程中的否决点数量、改革的社会回报以及补偿改革中的失利者的成本的影响，然后使用我们的新数据集来测试这个模型。我们发现，较富裕的国家既有更高的改革尝试率，也有更高的成功率，但与较贫穷的国家相比，个别改革对监管的影响较小。如果较富裕的国家在改革方面做得更好，这些发现与模型一致，可能是因为他们可以更有效地补偿失利者。在全世界范围内，改革尝试率与改革成功率强烈相关，但与改革影响水平无关。在国家内部，与行政或法律改革相比，更高比例的技术改革尝试是成功的，这与否决点的重要性一致。

### 法西斯主义崛起中的滞后效应与选择：纳粹党的“普通人”
**[Hysteresis and Selection in the Rise of Fascism: The ‘Ordinary Men’ of the Nazi Party](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35120)**

### 比较优势与殖民垄断：独家贸易公司的政治经济学
**[Comparative Advantage and Colonial Monopoly: The Political Economy of Exclusive Trading Companies](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35121)**

**Authors**: Sebastian Galiani, Ivan Lopez Cruz, Alessandra A. Palazzo, Gustavo Torrens

**Abstract**: This paper analyzes a central colonial institution: the monopolization of foreign trade through exclusive trading companies. We interpret this arrangement as an economic institution that creates a wedge between domestic and international prices—effectively an export tax—allowing metropolitan intermediaries to extract rents from colonial trade. Our primary theoretical contribution demonstrates that the economic and political consequences of this institutional form depend systematically on the structure of comparative advantage between the metropolis and the colony. While colonial export taxation unambiguously worsens the colony’s effective terms of trade, its effect on the metropolis is conditional. When the colony and metropolis are competitors—sharing a comparative advantage in the same sector—a colonial export tax can improve the metropolis’s terms of trade. **摘要**: 本文分析了一个核心的殖民机构：通过专属贸易公司垄断对外贸易。我们将这种安排解释为一种经济制度，它在国内外价格之间创造了一个楔子——实际上是出口税——允许大都市中介从殖民地贸易中提取租金。我们的主要理论贡献表明，这种制度形式的经济和政治后果系统地取决于大都市和殖民地之间的比较优势结构。尽管殖民地出口征税无疑恶化了殖民地的有效贸易条件，但其对大都市的影响是有条件的。当殖民地和大都市是竞争者——在同一个部门共享比较优势时——殖民地出口税可以改善大都市的贸易条件。相反，当它们是互补的——在不同部门专业化时——同样的政策可以通过提高进口投入的成本来恶化大都市的贸易条件。因为这些贸易条件的变化产生了不同的分配效应，殖民垄断重塑了大都市内的政治联盟，影响专属贸易公司是否持续或被解散。我们在简约的一般均衡模型中形式化了这一机制，并通过英国、印度和东印度公司（EIC）的历史经验来说明这一点，将EIC呈现为更广泛制度逻辑的一个显著实例。

**Abstract**: We study the resilience of payment systems to large disruptions in digital infrastructure caused by natural disasters and outages.
