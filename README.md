## 文章概览

### 移民、创新与增长的地理学
**[Immigration, Innovation, and the Geography of Growth](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35392)**

**Authors**: Costas Arkolakis, Sun Kyoung Lee, Michael Peters

**Abstract**: Between 1880 and 1920, more than 20 million immigrants settled in the United States. We study how this migration wave affected innovation and growth. Using a newly constructed dataset linking individual census records to historical immigration records and the universe of US patents, we highlight a new channel through which immigrants contributed to growth: they disproportionately settled in urban innovation hubs. To quantify the aggregate and regional effects of this mass migration episode, we develop a new spatial growth model in which skilled workers have a comparative advantage in innovation and sort endogenously across space. We find that international arrivals after 1880 raised US income per capita by 8.2% by 1940. Removing the subsequent immigration restrictions of the 1920s would have raised income per capita by a further 1.7% by 2000. Immigrants' skill composition and their concentration in urban hubs are key drivers of these effects.

**摘要**: 在1880年到1920年间，超过2000万移民在美国定居。我们研究了这股移民潮如何影响创新和增长。利用一个新构建的数据集，将个体人口普查记录与历史移民记录和美国专利全集联系起来，我们强调了移民对增长的一个新贡献渠道：他们不成比例地定居在城市创新中心。为了量化这次大规模移民潮的总体和地区效应，我们开发了一个新的空间增长模型，其中技术工人在创新方面具有比较优势，并在空间上内生地进行排序。我们发现，1880年后的国际移民到1940年将美国人均收入提高了8.2%。如果取消随后1920年代的移民限制，到2000年人均收入将进一步提高1.7%。移民的技能构成和他们在城市中心的集中是这些效应的关键驱动因素。

### 财富不平等与安全资产需求
**[Wealth Inequality and Safe Asset Demand](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35393)**

**Authors**: Xuning Ding, Zhengyang Jiang

**Abstract**: We study how wealth inequality shapes safe-asset demand in heterogeneous-agent economies. Asset bubbles arise when agents face sufficiently high probabilities of falling into extreme poverty. In such cases, assets insulated from idiosyncratic risk become infinitely large relative to agents' wealth ex-post, which makes them infinitely valuable ex-ante. This mechanism is fundamentally different from classic rational bubbles, generates bubbles even under stationary wealth distributions, and requires no aggregate uncertainty or growth. Using this insight, we revisit the pricing of aggregate assets in standard heterogeneous-agent economies, and show that prior analyses may be incomplete due to overlooked possibility of transversality condition violation.

**摘要**: 我们研究了财富不平等如何在异质性代理经济中塑造安全资产需求。当代理人面临足够高的概率陷入极端贫困时，资产泡沫就会浮现。在这种情况下，免受特有风险影响的资产相对于代理人事后的财富变得无限大，这使得它们在事前变得无限有价值。这种机制与经典的理性泡沫根本不同，即使在财富分布保持不变的情况下也能产生泡沫，并且不需要总体不确定性或增长。利用这一洞见，我们重新审视了标准异质性代理经济中总体资产的定价，并展示了由于忽视了横截性条件违反的可能性，先前的分析可能是不完整的。

### 为安全驾驶付费：肯尼亚小巴车行为和习惯的证据
**[Safer Driving for a Price: Evidence on Behavior and Habits in Kenyan Minibuses](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35394)**

**Authors**: David Schönholzer, Gregory Lane, Erin M. Kelley

**Abstract**: Road traffic injuries are the leading cause of death among people aged 5–29. Where traffic laws are weakly enforced, it is unclear whether road safety interventions can change behavior. We run a randomized controlled trial among 203 Kenyan minibuses, testing whether an incentive scheme improves safety overall or merely shifts unsafe driving to unmonitored times. Drivers reduce speeding by 29 percent and harsh braking by 13 percent, improving a safety index by 0.096 standard deviations. Labor-supply and earnings effects are modest, with little displacement across times or places. Improvements fade quickly, suggesting lasting change requires sustained enforcement or stronger incentives.

**摘要**: 道路交通伤害是5至29岁人群中死亡的主要原因。在交通法规执行不力的地方，尚不清楚道路安全干预措施是否能够改变行为。我们在203辆肯尼亚小巴中进行了一项随机对照试验，测试激励机制是否能够整体提高安全性，或者仅仅是将不安全驾驶转移到未受监控的时间。司机们将超速行为减少了29%，猛烈刹车行为减少了13%，安全指数提高了0.096个标准差。劳动力供应和收入效应较小，几乎没有跨时间或地点的转移。改进很快消失，这表明持久的改变需要持续的执法或更强的激励措施。

### 人工智能与万维网的崩溃
**[AI and the Collapse of the www](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35344)**

**Authors**: Alex Chan

**Abstract**: This paper studies market design for generative AI intermediation. AI answer systems can improve user experience while diverting visits that finance publisher content and generate source-level quality signals. I show that an AI platform that underinternalizes future content reproduction retains too little referral traffic and can make costly open-web information subcritical, even with truthful content, accurate answers, and rational users. The mechanism can be self-reinforcing: less source-level measurement weakens conventional search, inducing further AI reliance. Sustainable repair requires replacing displaced revenue and deleted measurement through visitor-replacement royalties, audited provenance, human-information audits, and keystone-topic compensation.

**摘要**: 本文研究了生成性人工智能中介的市场设计。AI问答系统可以提升用户体验，同时转移资助出版商内容的访问量，并产生来源级别的质量信号。我展示了一个未能充分内部化未来内容复制的AI平台保留了太少的转介流量，并且可以使开放网络信息变得不划算，即使内容真实、答案准确、用户理性。这种机制可能是自我加强的：更少的来源级测量削弱了传统搜索，导致进一步依赖AI。可持续的修复需要通过访客替代版税、审核来源、人类信息审计和关键主题补偿来替代被取代的收入和被删除的测量。

### 金融压制下的政府融资成本
**[Government Funding Costs Under Financial Repression](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35391)**

**Authors**: Roberto Gómez-Cram, Howard Kung, Hanno Lustig, David Zeke

**Abstract**: We study the equilibrium effects of financial repression on government funding costs in an endowment economy with limited asset market participation. We show how a broad set of repression policies operates through a wedge in the Euler equation responsive to government size or by affecting fiscal redistribution between agents. Repression intensity is captured by a policy feedback rule that depends positively on net government spending. When fiscal policy is profligate and monetary policy accommodates, we show that such a repression policy raises bond values, reduces the inflationary cost of unfunded fiscal expansions, and lowers bond risk premia. Repression is not a free lunch for bondholders---they pay a lower inflation tax but also earn lower future real returns. When monetary policy does not accommodate fiscal policy, repression can provide stopgap funding for deficits, allowing the central bank to retain control over inflation while making government debt a hedge for fiscal inflation.

**摘要**: 我们研究了金融抑制对政府融资成本的均衡效应，研究对象是一个参与资产市场有限的禀赋经济体。我们展示了一系列广泛的抑制政策如何通过欧拉方程中的楔子对政府规模做出反应，或者通过影响财政在不同主体之间的再分配。抑制强度通过一个政策反馈规则来捕捉，该规则与净政府支出正相关。当财政政策挥霍无度且货币政策适应时，我们展示了这种抑制政策如何提高债券价值，降低无资金支持的财政扩张的通胀成本，并降低债券风险溢价。对于债券持有人来说，抑制并非免费午餐——他们支付的通胀税较低，但未来实际回报也较低。当货币政策不配合财政政策时，抑制可以为赤字提供临时资金，允许中央银行保持对通胀的控制，同时使政府债务成为财政通胀的对冲工具。

### 实践者指南：在经济史中使用大型语言模型和生成性人工智能
**[A Practitioner's Guide to Using Large Language Models and Generative AI in Economic History](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35374)**

**Authors**: Andreas Ferrara

**Abstract**: Large language models (LLMs) are lowering the entry barriers to working with exciting data sources that used to require strong data science skills, such as handwritten ledgers, text, images, or sound recordings. This guide provides an introduction for researchers who are new to LLMs. It sets out a step-by-step workflow for turning a research idea into working code and data, and describes the four main ways of interacting with an LLM: the chat window, editor-integrated assistants, agentic coding tools, and the API. It then works through the decisions a practitioner meets in sequence, beginning with whether an LLM is the right tool and whether the data are allowed to be sent to one, then how to select models, write prompts, manage context limits, and control costs, and finally how to validate, reproduce, document, and correct LLM-generated measures in regression settings. A review of recent research shows how these tools already extract, link, harmonize, and classify historical data at scale. Four worked examples with replication files illustrate the use of LLMs. They classify emotions in paintings, link census records without names, measure newspaper salience and sentiment around the 1882 Chinese Exclusion Act, and score the emotional delivery of Franklin D. Roosevelt's wartime speeches. The guide also condenses the workflow, the best-practice recommendations, and the preparation of replication packages into summary tables and checklists to aid applied economists.

**摘要**: 大型语言模型（LLMs）降低了与以往需要强大数据科学技能的激动人心的数据源合作的入门门槛，例如手写账本、文本、图像或声音记录。本指南为新接触LLMs的研究人员提供了一个介绍。它为将研究想法转化为工作代码和数据设定了一个逐步的工作流程，并描述了与LLM互动的四种主要方式：聊天窗口、编辑器集成助手、代理编码工具和API。然后，它按顺序通过实践者遇到的决策，从是否选择LLM作为正确的工具和数据是否允许发送到LLM开始，然后如何选择模型、编写提示、管理上下文限制和控制成本，最后如何在回归设置中验证、复制、记录和纠正LLM生成的度量。最近的研究综述显示了这些工具如何已经在大规模上提取、链接、协调和分类历史数据。四个带复制文件的工作示例说明了LLMs的使用。它们对绘画中的情感进行分类，将没有名字的人口普查记录进行链接，测量1882年《排华法案》周围的报纸显著性和情感，并评估富兰克林·D·罗斯福战时演讲的情感表达。该指南还将工作流程、最佳实践建议和复制包的准备浓缩成摘要表和清单，以帮助应用经济学家。

### 补偿与强化：实验识别父母对子女不平等的厌恶
**[Compensation vs. Reinforcement: Experimental Identification of Parental Aversion to Inequality in Offspring](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35375)**

**Authors**: Felipe Barrera-Osorio, Leonardo Bonilla-Mejía, Matias Busso, Sebastian Galiani, Hyunjae Kang, Juan S. Muñoz-Morales, Juan Pantano

**Abstract**: Parents may invest differently across children by compensating the disadvantaged child or by reinforcing the child with higher expected returns. We study this question using a conditional cash transfer experiment that uniquely randomized transfers at the student level, generating exogenous variation in transfer exposure across siblings within the same household. The transfers increased short-run attendance among treated students but generated negative spillovers on untreated siblings: untreated siblings of treated students were 3.7 percentage points less likely to graduate from college, a decline of about 30 percent relative to the control mean. We interpret these effects using a dynamic model of household schooling decisions that identifies parental aversion to inequality in children’s educational outcomes. The estimated model implies limited aversion to inequality in children’s educational outcomes and reproduces held-out treatment effects not used in estimation. A decomposition shows that the negative spillover is primarily driven by substitution in educational investments toward the treated child.

**摘要**: 父母可能会通过补偿条件较差的孩子或加强预期回报更高的孩子来不同地投资孩子。我们使用一个有条件的现金转移实验来研究这个问题，该实验在学生层面随机分配转移，在同一家庭内为兄弟姐妹之间的转移暴露创造了外生变化。转移增加了接受治疗学生的短期出勤率，但对未接受治疗的兄弟姐妹产生了负面溢出效应：未接受治疗的兄弟姐妹完成大学学业的可能性比接受治疗的学生低3.7个百分点，相对于对照组平均值下降了约30%。我们使用一个动态的家庭学校决策模型来解释这些效应，该模型识别了父母对孩子教育结果不平等的厌恶。估计的模型意味着对孩子教育结果不平等的厌恶有限，并再现了未用于估计的保留治疗效果。一个分解表明，负面溢出主要是由于教育投资向接受治疗的孩子的替代。

### 重访价格与数量：政策制定者需要知道什么才能设定庇古税和补贴？
**[Prices Versus Quantities Revisited: What Do Policymakers Need to Know to Set Pigouvian Taxes and Subsidies?](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35376)**

**Authors**: Denise Dipasquale, Edward L. Glaeser, Adam M. Guren, Paul S. Willen

**Abstract**: What information do policymakers need to design Pigouvian taxes or subsidies? Standard logic suggests that it is sufficient to know the size of the externality and unnecessary to know about quantities. Yet this logic is incorrect if interventions have fixed costs, taxes create deadweight losses, or there are distributional concerns. We present a model in which these considerations can make it more valuable for policymakers to learn about equilibrium quantities. We apply the model to congestion pricing, which has high fixed costs, and to a proposed housing subsidy in Boston that features deadweight losses and distributional concerns.

**摘要**: 政策制定者需要哪些信息来设计庇古税或补贴？标准逻辑认为，只需知道外部性的大小，而无需了解数量。然而，如果干预有固定成本，税收会造成无谓损失，或者存在分配问题，这种逻辑就是错误的。我们提出了一个模型，在该模型中，这些考虑因素可能使政策制定者更有价值地了解均衡数量。我们将模型应用于拥堵收费，这具有高固定成本，以及波士顿提出的住房补贴，后者涉及无谓损失和分配问题。

### 对关税的劳动力市场反应：摩擦、动态和政策响应
**[Labor Market Responses to Tariffs: Frictions, Dynamics, and Policy Responses](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35377)**

**Authors**: Rafael Dix-Carneiro, Brian K. Kovak

**Abstract**: This article introduces the evidence and associated modeling frameworks contemporary economists use to understand the effects of trade and trade policy on labor markets, with a particular emphasis on labor-market frictions and adjustment dynamics.  The effects of trade shocks differ across industries, regions, and occupations, implying the presence of important adjustment frictions in labor markets, and these effects evolve slowly over time, implying the need for dynamic frameworks rationalizing slow transitions.  After reviewing the key insights from this literature, we discuss policies aimed at mitigating costs to workers and ensuring that the gains from trade are shared more equitably.

**摘要**: 本文介绍了当代经济学家用来理解贸易和贸易政策对劳动力市场影响的证据和相关建模框架，特别强调劳动力市场的摩擦和调整动态。贸易冲击对不同行业、地区和职业的影响不同，这意味着劳动力市场存在重要的调整摩擦，而这些影响随时间缓慢演变，意味着需要动态框架来合理化缓慢的过渡。在回顾了这些文献的关键见解之后，我们讨论了旨在减轻工人成本并确保贸易收益更公平共享的政策。

### 矩匹配的惊人灵活性
**[The Incredible Flexibility of Moment Matching](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35378)**

**Authors**: Isaiah Andrews, Bas Sanders

**Abstract**: We ask how far the choice of which moments to match can push estimates in misspecified structural models. The answer is: very far. Under regularity conditions, an adversarial researcher informed about the data distribution can choose moments that render any parameter value the unique solution to the population moment-matching problem. Moreover, in many cases they can do so with little increase in model-implied standard errors relative to maximum likelihood. We illustrate both results in a menu-cost model.

**摘要**: 我们探讨了在误设定的结构模型中，选择匹配哪些矩对估计值的影响能有多大。答案是：非常大。在一定条件下，一个了解数据分布的敌对研究者可以选择那些使得任何参数值成为总体矩匹配问题的唯一解的矩。而且，在许多情况下，他们可以在相对于最大似然估计的标准误差增加很少的情况下做到这一点。我们在一个菜单成本模型中展示了这两个结果。

### 对抗通货膨胀的经历
**[Leaning Against Inflation Experiences](https://www.nber.org/papers/w35379)**

**Authors**: Stefan Nagel

**Abstract**: A large share of secular variation in real interest rates can be understood as the effect of monetary policy leaning against experience-based long-run inflation expectations. Survey microdata show that adaptive learning from experienced inflation generates highly persistent, slow-moving long-run inflation expectations. When expectations are shaped by experience, central banks cannot anchor them through communication. Instead, when expectations deviate from the inflation target, monetary policy must remain persistently hawkish or dovish to generate realized inflation outcomes that, through agents’ belief updating, gradually pull long-run expectations back toward the target. Consistent with this mechanism, I find a strong positive relationship between experience-based long-run inflation expectations and real interest rates in the U.S., Germany, the U.K., and Japan. Under their subjective expectations, private-sector agents do not anticipate future reversals in inflation and short-term real interest rates.
