### 在领域内和跨领域的信念更新中的偏见
**[Biases in Belief Updating Within and Across Domains](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34638)**

**Authors**: Francesca Bastianello, Alex Imas

**Abstract**: Why do people sometimes overreact to new information and other times underreact? We develop a model in which the strength of a signal—how much one should update their beliefs with new information—depends on multiple features of the information environment. Limited attention to these features leads to misperceptions of signal strength: people approach a problem with an experience-based prior, which they adjust only partially based on how much attention they pay to different features. This mechanism explains a wide range of belief-updating patterns. Insensitivity to a single feature generates underreaction to strong and overreaction to weak signals, and more neglected features amplify this. Insensitivity to multiple features can instead break that pattern: insensitivity to one feature can generate excess sensitivity with respect to another, leading to overreaction to both weak and strong signals. A series of experiments provides support for the model and its underlying mechanism.

**摘要**: 为什么人们有时对新信息反应过度，而有时反应不足？我们开发了一个模型，其中信号的强度——即一个人应该根据新信息更新信念的程度——取决于信息环境的多个特征。对这些特征的有限关注导致了对信号强度的误解：人们带着基于经验的先验知识来处理问题，并且只根据他们对不同特征的关注程度进行部分调整。这种机制解释了广泛的信仰更新模式。对单一特征的不敏感会导致对强信号反应不足和对弱信号反应过度，而更多被忽视的特征会放大这一点。对多个特征的不敏感可以打破这种模式：对一个特征的不敏感可能会产生对另一个特征的过度敏感，导致对弱信号和强信号都反应过度。一系列实验为该模型及其背后的机制提供了支持。

### O型圈自动化
**[O-Ring Automation](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34639)**

**Authors**: Joshua S. Gans, Avi Goldfarb

**Abstract**: We study automation when tasks are quality complements rather than separable. Production requires numerous tasks whose qualities multiply as in an O-ring technology. A worker allocates a fixed endowment of time across the tasks performed; machines can replace tasks with given quality, and time is allocated across the remaining manual tasks. This “focus” mechanism generates three results. First, task-by-task substitution logic is incomplete because automating one task changes the return to automating others. Second, automation decisions are discrete and can require bundled adoption even when automation quality improves smoothly. Third, labour income can rise under partial automation because automation scales the value of remaining bottleneck tasks. These results imply that widely-used exposure indices, which aggregate task-level automation risk using linear formulas, will overstate displacement when tasks are complements. The relevant object is not average task exposure but the structure of bottlenecks and how automation reshapes worker time around them.

**摘要**: 我们研究的是在任务是质量互补而非可分离的情况下的自动化问题。生产需要众多任务，其质量像O型环技术一样相乘。工人在执行的任务之间分配固定的时间为禀赋；机器可以替代具有给定质量的任务，并且时间在剩余的手工任务之间分配。这种“专注”机制产生了三个结果。首先，逐项替代逻辑是不完整的，因为自动化一个任务会改变自动化其他任务的回报。其次，自动化决策是离散的，即使自动化质量平稳提高，也可能需要捆绑采用。第三，部分自动化下劳动收入可以增加，因为自动化扩大了剩余瓶颈任务的价值。这些结果意味着，广泛使用的风险暴露指数，使用线性公式汇总任务级别的自动化风险，会在任务是互补时高估替代。相关的对象不是平均任务暴露，而是瓶颈结构以及自动化如何围绕它们重新塑造工人的时间。

### 关税战冲击与美国国债的便利收益——一个对冲视角
**[Tariff War Shock and the Convenience Yield of US Treasuries — A Hedging Perspective](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34640)**

**Authors**: Viral V. Acharya, Toomas Laarits

**Abstract**: We explain how the “Tariff War” shock of April 2025 affected the safe-asset status of US Treasuries. Convenience yield erosion for long bonds is consistent with a reduction in the hedging property, reflected in a rising stock-bond covariance. Decomposing the Treasury yield into risk-free rate, credit spread, and convenience yield components reveals that covariance due to the convenience yield component increased for long bonds. The short end of the Treasury curve, however, continued to exhibit the safe-asset hedging property. These effects are consistent with a withdrawal of safe-asset investors from long-term Treasuries and a rotation towards shorter-term Treasuries and gold.

**摘要**: 我们解释了2025年4月的“关税战”冲击如何影响了美国国债的安全资产地位。长期债券的便利收益侵蚀与对冲属性的减弱一致，这反映在股票与债券协方差的上升上。将国债收益率分解为无风险利率、信用利差和便利收益部分，揭示了由于便利收益部分导致的长期债券的协方差增加。然而，国债曲线的短期端继续展现出安全资产的对冲属性。这些效应与安全资产投资者从长期国债撤出，转向短期国债和黄金是一致的。

### 重视粘性存款
**[Valuing Sticky Deposits](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34641)**

**Authors**: Matthias Fleckenstein, Shohini Kundu, Francis A. Longstaff

**Abstract**: We develop a formal fixed-income framework to value sticky deposits. This framework provides closed-form expressions for deposit values and allows us to study the impact of stickiness on deposit interest-rate and runoff risks. The duration of a bank deposit can be either positive or negative, which has important implications for hedging the interest-rate risk of bank balance sheets. Banks that maximize deposit value by following optimal deposit beta strategies may significantly increase their interest-rate and deposit runoff risks. We test the empirical implications of the valuation model using market deposit premia observed in bank merger/acquisition transactions. The results provide strong empirical support for the model’s predictions.

**摘要**: 我们开发了一个正式的固定收益框架来评估粘性存款的价值。该框架为存款价值提供了封闭形式的表达式，并允许我们研究粘性对存款利率和流失风险的影响。银行存款的期限可以是正的也可以是负的，这对于对冲银行资产负债表的利率风险具有重要意义。通过遵循最优存款beta策略来最大化存款价值的银行可能会显著增加它们的利率和存款流失风险。我们使用银行合并/收购交易中观察到的市场存款溢价来测试估值模型的经验含义。结果为模型的预测提供了强有力的经验支持。

### 一种工具，多种治疗效果：工具变量识别多重因果效应
**[One Instrument, Many Treatments: Instrumental Variables Identification of Multiple Causal Effects](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34607)**

**Authors**: Joshua Angrist, Andres Santos, Otávio Tecchio

**Abstract**: Many instrumental variables applications specify a single Bernoulli treatment. But instruments may change outcomes through multiple pathways or by varying treatment intensity. Lottery instruments that boost charter school enrollment, for instance, may affect outcomes by lengthening time enrolled in a charter school and by moving students between charter schools of different types. We analyze the identification problem such scenarios present in a framework that generalizes the always-taker/never-taker/complier partition of treatment response types to cover a wide range of multinomial and ordered treatments with heterogenous potential outcomes. This framework yields novel estimators in which a single randomly assigned instrument identifies (i) causal effects averaged over complier types and (ii) a causal conditional expectation function that captures effects for each element in a set of response types. Three empirical applications demonstrate the utility of these results. The first extends an earlier analysis of the Head Start Impact Study allowing for multiple fallbacks. The second examines two causal channels for the impact of post-secondary financial aid on degree completion. The third estimates effects of additional births (an ordered treatment) on mothers’ earnings.

**摘要**: 许多工具变量应用指定了一个单一的伯努利处理。但工具可能通过多个途径改变结果，或者通过变化处理强度来影响结果。例如，增加特许学校入学率的彩票工具可能通过延长在特许学校注册的时间以及将学生转移到不同类型的特许学校来影响结果。我们在一种框架内分析了这类场景所呈现的识别问题，该框架将总是接受者/从不接受者/遵从者的划分推广到涵盖广泛的多项式和有序处理以及具有异质性潜在结果的治疗反应类型。这个框架产生了新的估计器，其中一个随机分配的工具识别了（i）在遵从者类型上平均的因果效应和（ii）一个因果条件期望函数，该函数捕捉了一组反应类型中每个元素的效果。三个实证应用展示了这些结果的实用性。第一个扩展了早期对“头开始影响研究”的分析，允许有多个备选方案。第二个检查了高等教育财政援助对学位完成影响的两个因果渠道。第三个估计了额外生育（一个有序处理）对母亲收入的影响。

### 孟买地铁在印度的好处是什么？
**[What Are the Benefits of Metro Rail in Mumbai, India?](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34650)**

**Authors**: Palak Suri, Maureen L. Cropper

**Abstract**: We estimate the effects of the first metro rail line in Mumbai using administrative data on assessed property prices from 2011-18 for 723 subzones in the city. Comparing areas within 1 km of the metro with those beyond 1 km but within 3 km, we estimate the effects on property values for commercial, industrial, and residential properties. We find a significant and persistent increase in prices of 6-8% for residential and commercial land use categories in treated areas relative to control areas after Metro Line 1 opens. We show that commute time savings and improvements in employment accessibility are plausible mechanisms underlying these effects.

**摘要**: 我们使用2011-2018年孟买市723个分区的行政房产评估价格数据，估计了孟买第一条地铁线路的影响。比较地铁1公里范围内和1公里外但3公里内的区域，我们估计了地铁对商业、工业和住宅地产价值的影响。我们发现，在地铁1号线开通后，与对照区域相比，处理区域的住宅和商业用地类别的价格上涨了6-8%，且这种增长是显著且持续的。我们展示了通勤时间节省和就业可达性改善是这些效应背后可能的机制。

### 全球供应链的质量升级：哥伦比亚咖啡的证据
**[Quality Upgrading in Global Supply Chains: Evidence from Colombian Coffee](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34610)**

**Authors**: Rocco Macchiavello, Josepa Miquel-Florensa, Nicolas de Roux, Eric Verhoogen, Mario Bernasconi, Patrick W. Farrell

**Abstract**: Do the returns to quality upgrading pass through supply chains to primary producers? We explore this question in the context of Colombia’s coffee sector, in which market outcomes depend on interactions between farmers, exporters (which operate mills), and international buyers, and contracts are for the most part not legally enforceable. We formalize the hypothesis that quality upgrading is subject to a key hold-up problem: producing high-quality beans requires long-term investments by farmers, but there is no guarantee that an exporter will pay a quality premium when the beans arrive at its mills. An international buyer with sufficient demand for high-quality coffee can solve this problem by imposing a vertical restraint on the exporter, requiring the exporter to pay a quality premium to farmers. Combining internal records from two exporters, comprehensive administrative data, and the staggered rollout of a buyer-driven quality-upgrading program, we find empirical support for the key theoretical predictions, both the lack of pass-through of quality premia under normal circumstances and the possibility of a buyer-driven solution through a vertical restraint. Calibration of the model suggests that one-third to two-thirds of the (substantial) gains from the program accrue to farmers, with the vertical restraint playing a critical role. The results are consistent with the hypotheses that quality upgrading can provide a path to higher incomes for farmers, but also that it is unlikely to be viable under standard market conditions in the sector.

**Authors**: Ming Gu, David Hirshleifer, Siew Hong Teoh, Shijia Wu

**Abstract**: We study dynamic visual representations as a proxy for investor sentiment about the stock market. Our sentiment index, GIFsentiment, is constructed from millions of posts in the Graphics Interchange Format (GIF) on a leading investment social media platform. ### 培养企业家：在商业技能培训中对青年进行长期投资的回报
**[Making Entrepreneurs: Long Term Returns to Training Youth in Business Skills](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34637)**

**Authors**: Laura Chioda, Paul Gertler, David Contreras-Loya, Dana R. Carney

**Abstract**: We study the medium and long term impacts of Skills for Effective Entrepreneurship Development (SEED), a 3-week entrepreneurship training program for secondary school students in Uganda. The mini-MBA, modeled after business school curricula, was implemented as a randomized field experiment with a nationally representative sample of 4,402 youth. After four years, the training improved both hard and soft skills. SEED graduates became more effective negotiators and communicators and exhibited improved self-efficacy, stability, plasticity, and stress management. In the medium run, treated youth were more likely to start enterprises and more successful in ensuring their survival, thereby gaining greater entrepreneurial experience. Their ventures were also of higher quality: more likely to be formal, have employees, be in collaboration with other entrepreneurs, and use effective business management practices. With 52% of the sample still enrolled in post-secondary education, we find suggestive evidence that businesses led by the treatment groups performed better. After nine years, business ownership converged between treatment and control groups as control ownership rates doubled. However, SEED graduates maintained their edge in terms of business quality and operated firms with 20% higher revenues and 16% higher profits, without corresponding increases in capital or labor inputs, consistent with higher total factor productivity. Entrepreneurial success was achieved through the adoption of better business practices and experimentation, with soft skills related to entrepreneurial mindset playing a complementary role. SEED generated high returns on investment: the present discounted values of SEED-induced business and total earnings equal 20 and 27 times program costs, respectively.

**摘要**: 我们研究了“有效创业发展技能”（SEED）项目对乌干达中学生的中长期影响，这是一个为期3周的创业培训项目。这个迷你MBA课程模仿商学院课程，以随机田野实验的形式实施，样本覆盖了全国代表性的4,402名青年。四年后，该培训提高了硬技能和软技能。SEED项目的毕业生成为了更有效的谈判者和沟通者，并表现出提高的自我效能、稳定性、可塑性和压力管理能力。在中期内，接受培训的青年更有可能创办企业，并在确保企业生存方面更成功，从而获得了更多的创业经验。他们的企业质量也更高：更有可能正式注册、拥有员工、与其他创业者合作，并采用有效的商业管理实践。在样本中有52%的人仍在接受高等教育，我们发现有迹象表明，由接受培训的群体领导的企业表现更好。九年后，处理组和对照组之间的企业所有权趋于一致，因为对照组的所有权率翻了一番。然而，SEED项目的毕业生在企业质量方面保持了优势，他们运营的企业收入高出20%，利润高出16%，而没有相应的资本或劳动力投入增加，这与更高的全要素生产率一致。创业成功是通过采用更好的商业实践和实验实现的，与创业心态相关的软技能发挥了补充作用。SEED项目投资回报率高：由SEED项目引发的商业和总收入的现值分别是项目成本的20倍和27倍。

### 从治疗到安全：物质使用治疗在预防亲密伴侣暴力中的作用
**[From Treatment to Safety: The Role of Substance Use Treatment in Preventing Intimate Partner Violence](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34642)**

**Authors**: Mevlude Akbulut-Yuksel, Dhaval M. Dave, Bilge Erten, Pinar Keskin, Catarina R. Meneses

**Abstract**: Substance use is a well-established driver of intimate partner violence (IPV), with drug-related incidents posing persistent challenges for both public health and criminal justice systems. We examine how expanding access to substance use treatment (SUT) services affects IPV in the United States by leveraging variation in the opening and closing of treatment facilities at the county level. Using administrative data on IPV incidents from the National Incident-Based Reporting System at the agency level from 1998 to 2019 combined with county-level records on treatment facility information, we implement a continuous difference-in-differences research design. **摘要**: 物质使用是亲密伴侣暴力（IPV）的一个已确立的驱动因素，与毒品相关的事件对公共卫生和刑事司法系统都构成了持续的挑战。我们通过利用县级治疗设施的开设和关闭的变化，来检验扩大获得物质使用治疗（SUT）服务如何影响美国的IPV。我们使用了来自国家事件基础报告系统（NIBRS）的1998年至2019年的机构级别IPV事件的行政数据，以及县级治疗设施信息的记录，实施了一个连续的差异-差异研究设计。我们的结果显示，在每个县平均每年增加三个SUT设施，可以减少涉及毒品的IPV约1.5-1.7%。我们没有发现对酒精相关或非物质相关的IPV有显著影响的证据。分阶段的事件研究分析证实了在净设施开设之前，经过处理和未经处理的县之间的结果趋势是平行的，这支持了对估计值的因果解释。来自治疗事件数据集（TEDS）的SUT入院相关证据显示，新中心显著提高了治疗入院率，特别是在男性中，这与减少施暴行为推动观察到的IPV暴露下降相一致。我们的发现强调了卫生服务基础设施在塑造与暴力相关的结果中的作用，并强调了投资治疗获取的更广泛的公共安全利益。

### 为富人统治：最高法院随时间的变化
**[Ruling for the Rich: the Supreme Court over Time](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34643)**

**Authors**: Andrea Prat, Fiona Scott Morton, Jacob Spitz

**Abstract**: To investigate the emergence of a pro-wealthy bias in the US Supreme Court, we develop a protocol to identify and analyze all cases involving economic issues from 1953 to the present. We categorize the parties in these cases as “rich” or “poor” according to their likelihood of being wealthy. A vote is pro-rich if that outcome would directly shift resources to the party that is more likely to be wealthy. Using this dataset, we estimate case-specific intercepts, justice-specific latent ideal points, and party-level time trends using the Bayesian methods pioneered by Martin and Quinn (2002). ### 国库拍卖中的进入与退出
**[Entry and Exit in Treasury Auctions](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34646)**

**Authors**: Jason Allen, Ali Hortaçsu, Eric Richert, Milena Wittwer

**Abstract**: Hedge funds have become increasingly active in Treasury markets, yet we have little  evidence on how this shift has affected the market. We focus on the primary market, where the government issues its debt through auctions. Using over 20 years of  data from Canadian Treasury auctions, we document dealers exiting and hedge funds entering. ### 市场交易的道德性：社会价值与权衡之间的问题
**[The Morality of Market Exchanges: Between Societal Values and Tradeoffs](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34647)**

**Authors**: Julio J. Elias, Nicola Lacetera, Mario Macis

**Abstract**: Certain behaviors in markets are unambiguously unethical. In other cases, however, voluntary exchanges that can create gains from trade remain contested on moral grounds, because of what is traded or of the price at which the exchange occurs. This chapter offers a framework to analyze these contested markets and provides examples of two general instances. ### 工具变量回归中的贝叶斯推断
**[Bayesian Inference in IV Regressions](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34648)**

**Authors**: Domenico Giannone, Michele Lenza, Giorgio Primiceri

**Abstract**: It is well known that standard frequentist inference breaks down in IV regressions with weak instruments. Bayesian inference with diffuse priors suffers from the same problem. We show that the issue arises because flat priors on the first-stage coefficients overstate instrument strength. In contrast, inference improves drastically when an uninformative prior is specified directly on the concentration parameter—the key nuisance parameter capturing instrument relevance. The resulting Bayesian credible intervals are asymptotically equivalent to the frequentist confidence intervals based on conditioning approaches, and remain robust to weak instruments.

**摘要**: 众所周知，在具有弱工具变量的IV回归中，标准的频率主义推断会失效。带有分散先验的贝叶斯推断也存在同样的问题。我们表明，这个问题出现的原因是，对第一阶段系数的平坦先验高估了工具变量的强度。相比之下，当直接在集中参数上指定一个非信息性先验时，推断会大幅改善——这个关键的干扰参数捕捉了工具变量的相关性。由此产生的贝叶斯可信区间在渐近上等同于基于条件方法的频率主义置信区间，并且对弱工具变量保持稳健。

### 财政不作为作为货币支持
**[Fiscal Inaction as Monetary Support](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34654)**

**Authors**: George-Marios Angeletos, Chen Lian, Christian K. Wolf

**Abstract**: How does the fiscal framework affect the central bank's ability to stabilize output and inflation? The textbook answer, which assumes Ricardian households, recommends that fiscal adjustment should be fast enough to allow for monetary dominance. We instead argue that, with non-Ricardian households, the central bank may indeed welcome slow, or even no, fiscal adjustment. On the demand side, slow fiscal adjustment helps stabilize aggregate spending; on the supply side, it eases tax distortions, improving the output-inflation trade off. And while the first channel favors slow fiscal adjustment only when the business cycle is dominated by demand shocks, the second channel extends this preference to supply shocks. A quantitative exercise affirms our lessons in the U.S. context, with the central bank preferring virtually no fiscal adjustment over the business cycle.

**摘要**: 财政框架如何影响中央银行稳定产出和通胀的能力？教科书的答案，假设家庭是李嘉图等价的，建议财政调整应该足够快，以允许货币主导。我们反而认为，对于非李嘉图家庭来说，中央银行实际上可能欢迎缓慢的，甚至没有财政调整。在需求方面，缓慢的财政调整有助于稳定总支出；在供给方面，它减轻了税收扭曲，改善了产出通胀权衡。尽管第一个渠道只在商业周期由需求冲击主导时才倾向于缓慢的财政调整，但第二个渠道将这种偏好扩展到供给冲击。一个定量练习在美国背景下证实了我们的教训，中央银行在商业周期中几乎不倾向于任何财政调整。
