### 智能电力限制：为极端事件设计短缺机制
**[Smart Power Limits: Designing Shortage Mechanisms for Extreme Events](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34196)**

**Authors**: Mar Reguant, Mayra Wagner

**Abstract**: As shortages of resources like water and electricity due to extreme weather events become more frequent, high prices alone may fail to curb demand, making shortages more common. We examine a power limit policy for residential electricity households that rations electricity consumption rather than setting it to zero, as in a traditional rolling blackout. We find that power limits can provide equivalent savings to large blackouts, even when generous. Additionally, due to selection, power limits reduce the number of households affected by a shortage event. We conclude by discussing the welfare consequences of power limits and their heterogeneous impact across households.

**摘要**: 随着极端天气事件导致水资源和电力等资源短缺变得越来越频繁，仅靠高价格可能无法抑制需求，从而使短缺变得更加普遍。我们研究了一种针对住宅电力用户的电力限制政策，该政策对电力消费进行配给，而不是像传统轮流停电那样将其设置为零。我们发现，即使在慷慨的情况下，电力限制也能提供与大规模停电相当的节电效果。此外，由于选择性，电力限制减少了受短缺事件影响的家庭数量。我们最后讨论了电力限制的福利后果及其对不同家庭的异质性影响。

### 通过因果人+机器学习理解专利差异
**[Understanding Patenting Disparities via Causal Human+Machine Learning](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34197)**

**Authors**: Lin William Cong, Stephen Q. Yang

**Abstract**: We develop an empirical approach for analyzing multi-dimensional discrimination using multimodal data, combining human perception measures with language-embedding-based, nonlinear controls for latent quality to relax restrictive assumptions in causal machine learning. Applying it to the U.S. patent examination process, we find that, ceteris paribus, applications from female inventors are 1.8 percentage points less likely to be approved, and those from Black inventors are 3 percentage points less likely—inconsistent with legally prescribed criteria. Jointly studying multiple bias dimensions and their intersections for the first time, we uncover new biases, including an affiliation bias—individual inventors are disadvantaged by 6.6 percentage points relative to employees of large, public firms, a disparity larger than any demographic gap. Moreover, innovation quality, location, and other factors can mitigate or compound discrimination, and the disparities interact: for example, racial gaps vanish among public-firm employees, masking more severe discrimination against individuals. Existing theories such as homophily cannot fully explain the results, but a simple model of correlation neglect does.

**摘要**: 我们开发了一种实证方法，用于分析多维歧视问题，该方法结合了多模态数据，将人类感知度量与基于语言嵌入的非线性控制相结合，以放松因果机器学习中的限制性假设。将其应用于美国专利审查过程，我们发现，在其他条件相同的情况下，来自女性发明家的申请获批的可能性低1.8个百分点，而来自黑人发明家的申请获批的可能性低3个百分点——这与法律规定的标准不一致。我们首次联合研究多个偏见维度及其交叉点，发现了新的偏见，包括一种关联偏见——个体发明家相对于大型上市公司的员工处于劣势，差距为6.6个百分点，这一差距大于任何人口统计学上的差距。此外，创新质量、地点和其他因素可以减轻或加剧歧视，而这些差异是相互作用的：例如，在上市公司员工中，种族差距消失了，掩盖了对个体的更严重歧视。现有的理论，如同质性偏好，不能完全解释这些结果，但一个简单的相关性忽视模型可以。

### 失灵的民主：理解发展中国家问责失败
**[Malfunctioning Democracies: Understanding Accountability Failures in Developing Countries](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34198)**

**Authors**: Claudio Ferraz, Frederico Finan

**Abstract**: This chapter examines why democracies in the developing world frequently underperform in providing effective governance. We argue that these shortcomings stem from weaknesses in accountability mechanisms, which leave governments vulnerable to corruption, clientelism, and elite capture. Our framework distinguishes three accountability channels: vertical (citizens’ control over politicians), horizontal (checks and balances across state institutions), and diagonal (oversight by media and civil society). We synthesize the recent theoretical and empirical literature to assess how each channel operates, the conditions under which it succeeds, and why it often fails. A central finding is that accountability institutions rarely fail on their own; instead, they are actively undermined by political actors seeking to preserve rents and entrench power. This dynamic weakens electoral competition, erodes judicial independence, and curtails media freedom, producing a mutually reinforcing cycle of weak accountability. Additionally, we argue that sustainable reforms cannot be achieved by strengthening any single channel in isolation. Since vertical, horizontal, and diagonal accountability are interdependent, effective reform requires bolstering all three simultaneously. We conclude by discussing the implications of this perspective for future research, including the role of new technologies, political polarization, and candidate selection in reshaping accountability in developing democracies.

**摘要**: 本章探讨了发展中国家的民主政体在提供有效治理方面为何常常表现不佳。我们认为，这些不足源于问责机制的薄弱，使得政府容易受到腐败、裙带关系和精英俘获的影响。我们的框架区分了三个问责渠道：垂直（公民对政治家的控制）、水平（国家机构间的制衡）和对角线（媒体和民间社会的监督）。我们综合了最新的理论和实证文献，以评估每个渠道的运作方式、成功条件以及常常失败的原因。一个核心发现是，问责机构很少单独失败；相反，它们是被寻求维护租金和巩固权力的政治行为者积极破坏的。这种动态削弱了选举竞争，侵蚀了司法独立，并限制了媒体自由，产生了一个相互加强的弱问责循环。此外，我们认为，通过单独加强任何一个渠道是无法实现可持续改革的。由于垂直、水平和对角线问责是相互依赖的，有效的改革需要同时加强这三个渠道。我们最后讨论了这一视角对未来研究的影响，包括新技术、政治极化和候选人选拔在重塑发展中国家问责方面的作用。

### 在金钱中，我们生存：社会保障退休收入对寿命的影响
**[In Money, We Survive: The Effects of Social Security Retirement Income on Longevity](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34199)**

**Authors**: Hamid Noghanibehambari, Jason Fletcher

**Abstract**: An old and debated line of research examines the income-mortality relationship and finds mixed evidence. In this paper, we re-evaluate previous studies using a new dataset and implementing a difference-in-difference model based on a Notch in Social Security retirement benefits to overcome selection and endogeneity issues. We employ Social Security Administration death records and find a positive income-longevity relationship. Moreover, we find more pronounced effects among low-educated individuals and people from low socioeconomic status families. Analyses using census data suggest that part of the reductions in retirement income are offset by wage income due to post-retirement labor force participation. Past age 80, the net adverse effects of the policy on both income and longevity become more pronounced.

**摘要**: 一项古老且存在争议的研究领域探讨了收入与死亡率之间的关系，并发现了混合的证据。在本文中，我们使用一个新的数据集重新评估以往的研究，并实施了基于社会保障退休福利中的缺口（Notch）的差异-差异模型，以克服选择和内生性问题。我们利用社会保障管理局的死亡记录，并发现收入与长寿之间存在正相关关系。此外，我们发现在低教育水平的个体和来自低社会经济地位家庭的人中，这种效应更为明显。使用人口普查数据的分析表明，由于退休后劳动力参与而产生的工资收入，部分抵消了退休收入的减少。在80岁之后，该政策对收入和长寿的净负面影响变得更加明显。

### 超越泰勒规则
**[Beyond the Taylor Rule](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34200)**

**Authors**: Emi Nakamura, Venance Riblier, Jón Steinsson

**Abstract**: The Federal Reserve partially "looked through" the post-Covid rise in inflation and ultimately managed to bring about an "immaculate disinflation." The Fed's policy deviated strongly from the Taylor rule during this period. More generally, central banks with strong inflation-fighting credentials looked through post-Covid inflationary shocks yet experienced less inflation than more hawkish but less credible central banks. In light of this episode, we assess the degree to which the Taylor rule is descriptive, and the degree to which it should be viewed as prescriptive. While the Taylor rule (generally) fits well during the Greenspan period, it (generally) fits poorly in the early 1980s and after the early 2000s. Academic work has emphasized the role of the Taylor rule in preventing self-fulfilling fluctuations (guaranteeing determinacy). These concerns can be addressed with a shock-contingent commitment and are fragile to deviations from fully rational expectations. We discuss three reasons why optimal policy may not always imply a one-for-one response of interest rates to inflation (forward guidance, correlated shocks, and "long and variable lags"). The main challenge arising from such policies is not indeterminacy but erosion of inflation-fighting credibility and potential deanchoring of long-run inflation expectations. Only central banks with strongly anchored inflation expectations and large amounts of inflation-fighting credibility are likely to be able to look through inflationary shocks.

**摘要**: 美联储部分“忽视”了疫情后通胀的上升，并最终成功实现了“完美无瑕的通胀下降”。在此期间，美联储的政策严重偏离了泰勒规则。更普遍地说，拥有强烈抗击通胀信誉的中央银行忽视了疫情后的通胀冲击，但通胀水平却低于更为鹰派但信誉较低的中央银行。鉴于这一事件，我们评估了泰勒规则在多大程度上是描述性的，以及在多大程度上应该被视为规范性的。虽然泰勒规则（通常）在格林斯潘时期很好地契合，但它（通常）在20世纪80年代初和2000年代初之后契合度较差。学术工作强调了泰勒规则在防止自我实现的波动（保证确定性）中的作用。这些担忧可以通过一个受冲击影响的承诺来解决，并且对完全理性预期的偏离是脆弱的。我们讨论了三个原因，为什么最优政策并不总是意味着利率对通胀的一对一反应（前瞻性指引、相关冲击和“长而可变的时间滞后”）。这些政策带来的主要挑战不是不确定性，而是通胀抗击信誉的侵蚀和长期通胀预期的潜在脱锚。只有那些通胀预期稳定且拥有大量抗击通胀信誉的中央银行，才可能能够忽视通胀冲击。

### 经济研究的人工智能代理
**[AI Agents for Economic Research](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34202)**

**Authors**: Anton Korinek

**Abstract**: The objective of this paper is to demystify AI agents - autonomous LLM-based systems that plan, use tools, and execute multi-step research tasks - and to provide hands-on instructions for economists to build their own, even if they do not have programming expertise. As AI has evolved from simple chatbots to reasoning models and now to autonomous agents, the main focus of this paper is to make these powerful tools accessible to all researchers. Through working examples and step-by-step code, it shows how economists can create agents that autonomously conduct literature reviews across myriads of sources, write and debug econometric code, fetch and analyze economic data, and coordinate complex research workflows. The paper demonstrates that by "vibe coding" (programming through natural language) and building on modern agentic frameworks like LangGraph, any economist can build sophisticated research assistants and other autonomous tools in minutes. By providing complete, working implementations alongside conceptual frameworks, this guide demonstrates how to employ AI agents in every stage of the research process, from initial investigation to final analysis.

**摘要**: 本文的目标是揭开人工智能代理的神秘面纱——基于大型语言模型（LLM）的自主系统，它们能够规划、使用工具并执行多步骤的研究任务——并为经济学家提供构建自己代理的实用指南，即使他们没有编程专长。随着人工智能从简单的聊天机器人发展到推理模型，再到现在的自主代理，本文的主要重点是使这些强大的工具对所有研究人员都易于使用。通过工作示例和分步代码，本文展示了经济学家如何创建能够自主进行跨众多来源的文献综述、编写和调试计量经济学代码、获取和分析经济数据以及协调复杂研究工作流程的代理。本文展示了通过“氛围编码”（通过自然语言编程）和构建现代代理框架如LangGraph，任何经济学家都可以在几分钟内构建复杂的研究助手和其他自主工具。通过提供完整的、可工作的实现以及概念框架，本指南展示了如何在研究过程的每个阶段使用人工智能代理，从初步调查到最终分析。

### 消费者决策的离散选择分析：非法市场中的尼古丁产品
**[A Discrete Choice Analysis of Consumer Decisions: Nicotine Products in an Illicit Market](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34201)**

**Authors**: Asena Caner, Belgi Turan, Berna Tari Kasnakoğlu, Yenal Can Yiğit, Donald S. Kenkel, Alan D. Mathios

**Abstract**: This study investigates consumer stated preferences for manufactured cigarettes, roll-your-own tobacco, and vapes in Türkiye, with a focus on how product attributes shape choices of adult consumers. A discrete choice experiment embedded in an online survey examines the role of prices of these products, flavor availability, and most importantly the legal status of vapes. Results indicate strong price sensitivity, both to own prices and to the prices of substitutes. In addition, legal status emerges as a critical factor that shapes stated preferences: consumers exhibit a marked aversion to products that are banned or sold illegally. However, scenario analyses suggest that vapes would capture a substantial market share even under strict prohibition. The hypothetical scenario of a complete ban would likely have a modest effect on the cessation of nicotine products while shifting choices toward traditional combustible tobacco products.  These findings highlight the limits of prohibition and underscore the importance of regulatory design. In particular, the treatment of legal status, together with pricing and taxation policies, plays a decisive role in shaping consumer behavior and public health outcomes.

**摘要**: 本研究调查了土耳其消费者对制造型香烟、手卷烟和电子烟的声明偏好，重点关注产品属性如何塑造成年消费者选择。一个嵌入在线调查中的离散选择实验检验了这些产品的价格、口味可用性以及最重要的电子烟的法律地位的作用。结果表明，对自身价格和替代品价格都有强烈的价格敏感性。此外，法律地位作为一个关键因素出现，塑造了声明偏好：消费者对被禁止或非法销售的产品表现出明显的厌恶。然而，情景分析表明，即使在严格禁止的情况下，电子烟也会占据相当大的市场份额。完全禁止的假设情景可能会对尼古丁产品的停止使用产生适度影响，同时将选择转向传统的可燃烟草产品。这些发现突出了禁止的局限性，并强调了监管设计的重要性。特别是，法律地位的处理，连同定价和税收政策，在塑造消费者行为和公共卫生结果方面发挥着决定性作用。

### 企业所有权与污染
**[Firm Ownership and Pollution](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34203)**

### 动态环境中的最优性特征：基于单调性的途径
**[Characterizing Optimality in Dynamic Settings: A Monotonicity-based Approach](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34224)**

**Authors**: Zhuokai Huang, Demian Pouzo, Andrés Rodríguez-Clare

**Abstract**: We develop a novel analytical method for studying optimal paths in dynamic optimization problems under general monotonicity conditions. The method centers on a locator function—a simple object constructed directly from the model’s primitives—whose roots identify interior steady states and whose slope determines their local stability. Under strict concavity of the payoff function, the locator function also characterizes basins of attraction, yielding a complete description of qualitative dynamics. Without concavity, it can still deliver sharp results: if the function is single crossing from above, its root identifies a globally stable steady state; if the locator function is inverted-U-shaped with two interior roots (a typical case), only the higher root can be a locally stable interior steady state. The locator function further enables comparative statics of steady states with respect to parameters through direct analysis of its derivatives. These results are obtained without solving the full dynamic program. We illustrate the approach using a generalized neoclassical growth model, a rational (un)fitness model, and a learning-by-doing economy.

**摘要**: 我们开发了一种新的分析方法，用于在一般单调性条件下研究动态优化问题中的最优路径。该方法的核心是一个定位函数——一个直接从模型的基本元素构建的简单对象——其根识别内部稳态，其斜率决定了它们的局部稳定性。在收益函数严格凹的情况下，定位函数还描述了吸引域，从而提供了定性动态的完整描述。即使没有凹性，它仍然可以提供尖锐的结果：如果函数是从上方单交叉的，其根识别了一个全局稳定的稳态；如果定位函数是倒U形的，并且有两个内部根（一个典型情况），则只有较高的根可以是局部稳定的内部稳态。定位函数进一步通过直接分析其导数，使稳态相对于参数的比较静态分析成为可能。这些结果的获得无需解决完整的动态规划。我们使用一个广义的新古典增长模型、一个理性（非）适应性模型和一个通过实践学习的经济体来说明这种方法。

### 衡量国家间外国直接投资与贸易相互依存：以美国和中国为例
**[Measuring FDI and Trade-related Interdependence Across Countries: The Case of the United States and China](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34226)**

**Authors**: Zhi Wang, Shang-Jin Wei, Xinding Yu, Kunfu Zhu

**Abstract**: Based on the new integrated accounting framework of global supply chains (GSC) (Wang, Wei, Yu, and Zhu, 2025), we derive three types of economic dependence (producers’ dependence on upstream supplies, producers’ dependence on downstream markets, and consumers’ dependence on supply chains) that take into account of the presence of foreign invested firms as well as international trade. To measure indirect value flows between the origin and destination countries that are mediated through third economies, we show a need to go beyond both trade-in-value added and gross trade statistics. As an application, we derive new measures of inter-dependence between the United States and China.

**摘要**: 基于全球供应链（GSC）的新综合会计框架（王、魏、余和朱，2025年），我们推导出三种类型的经济依赖（生产者对上游供应的依赖、生产者对下游市场的依赖以及消费者对供应链的依赖），这些依赖考虑了外资企业和国际贸易的存在。为了衡量通过第三国中介的原产国和目的地之间的间接价值流动，我们展示了需要超越贸易增加值和总贸易统计数据的必要性。作为一个应用，我们推导出了美国和中国之间相互依赖的新度量。

### 收入不稳定性
**[Earnings Instability](https://www.nber.org/papers/w34227)**

**Authors**: Peter Ganong, Pascal J. Noel, Christina Patterson, Joseph S. Vavra, Alexander Weinberg

**Abstract**: This paper uses high-frequency administrative data to show that the majority of U.S. workers experience substantial month-to-month fluctuations in pay, even within ongoing employment relationships. This earnings instability is pervasive, but it has been masked in past analysis of annual data. Moreover, this instability is unequally distributed: lower-income, hourly workers face more instability than higher-income, salaried workers. This is because earnings instability arises in large part from firm-driven fluctuations in hours. This earnings instability is a meaningful source of economic risk: we provide causal evidence that it increases consumption volatility and also leads to greater job separations, and we find that workers have a high willingness to pay to reduce earnings instability. These findings suggest that short-term earnings risk is a significant and previously underappreciated feature of the labor market.

**摘要**: 本文利用高频行政数据表明，大多数美国工人即使在持续的雇佣关系中，也会经历工资的大幅月度波动。这种收入不稳定性普遍存在，但在过去对年度数据的分析中被掩盖了。此外，这种不稳定性分布不均：低收入、按小时计酬的工人比高收入、领薪水的工人面临更多的不稳定性。这是因为收入不稳定性很大程度上源于企业驱动的工时波动。这种收入不稳定性是一个重要的经济风险来源：我们提供了因果证据，表明它增加了消费波动，并导致更多的工作分离，我们发现工人愿意支付高昂的代价来减少收入不稳定性。这些发现表明，短期收入风险是劳动市场一个重要且以前被低估的特征。
